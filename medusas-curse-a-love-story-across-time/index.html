Little did Sophia know that her discovery would awaken a curse that had been hidden for millennia, entwining her fate with that of a legendary creature from Greek mythology - the Gorgon Medusa. As Sophia spent more time deciphering the inscription, she began to experience vivid visions and nightmares, hinting at the existence of a mystical realm hidden from mortal eyes. Her research led her to the Hermes Museum of Antiquities, where she collided with a charismatic yet enigmatic figure named Theo. He had been searching for Sophia, drawn by the very energies emanating from the artifact she'd uncovered. The air was charged with a palpable tension as the two strangers locked eyes, their connection sparking a mysterious force that transcended time and space.
