Chapter 5: The Price of Curiosity

The next morning, the townsfolk found Ethan standing alone on the cliffs, his eyes wide and unblinking, a faint, eerie smile playing on his lips. He was muttering to himself, repeating the same words over and over.

“I found it… I found it…”

But when they tried to speak to him, he was unresponsive, lost to the madness that had claimed so many before him. The treasure was gone, as was Thomas. Neither of them had been seen since.

The legend of Captain Blackthorn’s treasure lived on, but so too did the curse. For anyone who dared seek it would, in the end, pay the price.

And so, Fairwater remained, a town of stories and mysteries, haunted by the past and the shadows of those who sought what they could never have.
