The Forbidden Necromancy: A Supernatural Rebirth


In the depths of the ancient, forgotten forest of Eldervale, where the shadows whispered secrets and the winds carried tales of times long past, stood an old and crumbling mansion. It had been home to the revered necromancer Elara Nael, a legend among her people for her unparalleled mastery over the opus of life and death.

Ash and cobwebs covered every corner of the house, only the occasional flicker of candlelight visible through the dusty windows, inviting those daring - or foolish enough - to venture close. The vibrant life once pulsing through its walls had long since been drained away, leaving behind the cold, soulless husk that remained.

But not everyone feared the necromancer’s arts. There were those who sought her knowledge, those who yearned to tap into the unfathomable secrets she had stored in her heart. Among them was a prodigy named Novalyn, a brilliant sorceress of extraordinary potential, driven solely by an insatiable curiosity for esoteric knowledge and the desire to wield the power to control life and death.

Their chance came on a fateful summer night. With the stars hidden behind clouds, the world lay shrouded in darkness, a fitting time for clandestine acts and forbidden arts. Novalyn snuck into the heart of Eldervale under the cover of night, her eyes fixed on the dilapidated mansion where Elara Nael had once wielded her power over the veiled borders of life and death.

Gently, she traced her fingers over the ancient masonry, whispering the ancient words of invitation known only to the most learned necromancers, “Serothys Graevoth, Sachrara Eldervale.” A tremor passed through the ground, and the blackened gates creaked open. Novalyn steeled her heart, stepping forward into the unknown.

Within, the air thick with ethereal energy and the musty aroma of dried parchment, dusty tomes filled shelves from floor to ceiling. An ancient table stood haphazardly in the center of the room, covered in scrolls, bones, and unidentifiable relics. Atop this pile of artifacts rested an imposing tome, its ancient pages shimmering with the radiant aura of barely contained power.

Novalyn approached the book, fingers trembling with anticipation as she carefully lifted it. The instant her hands met the weathered leather, the enchanting energy of the tome flowed through her, awakening her long-dormant abilities and igniting a supernatural fire within her heart. Where once had lay nothing but skin and bone, now a living, beating heart resided in Novalyn’s chest.

The secrets Elara had unlocked within that tome now resided within Novalyn. With this newfound power vibrantly pulsing through her being, Novalyn began to unravel the mysteries of life and death, separating the essence of life from the hull of skeletal remains with divine precision.

Realizing the potential of her rekindled abilities, Novalyn felt herself being drawn to the necromancer’s workshop, where the most intimate practices of the dark arts took place. Embarking on a descent into forbidden knowledge, she unearthed the answers to elude the grasp of even the most scholarly of magi.

Soon, word spread about the prodigious necromancer who could summon the dead to serve her will. Kings and warlords alike sought Novalyn’s aid, the allure of military prowess hard to ignore. Soldiers risen from the dead fought with relentless, unyielding determination, and enemies laid fallen with no hope or chance of escape.

Though her newfound power granted Novalyn the greatest successes she had ever known, the weight of the dark arts took its toll on Novalyn’s heart and soul. In her pursuit for knowledge and control, she had strayed into a realm riddled with sorrow and ruin. For every person brought back from the dead, a piece of Novalyn’s own humanity slipped away, replaced by an overwhelming void of loss, pain, and suffering.

She contemplated the tragic fate that had befallen Elara Nael; how the forbidden arts had gripped the world by its own morality and reshaped it to its own accord. Viewing the effects her practices had on the world and her own well-being left her with no choice but to embark on a journey beyond the shadows of the past to unearth the truth.

With Elara’s tome dangling from the crook of her arm, Novalyn walked away from her dark abode, leaving behind the secrets she had once so fervently sought. Shaking off the ashes of her past, she set foot upon the path of her own destiny. For in the annals of Necromancy, only one who can fully comprehend the implications of that which lies beyond the veil of life and death can bear the title of master.

In the depths of the ancient, forgotten forest of Eldervale, the old and crumbling mansion stood silent once again, merely a monument to the history left behind for those daring enough to seek solace in its shadow.
