Ekaterina's Table: An Insider's Guide to Russian Cuisine


In the heart of Moscow, where the onion domes of ancient cathedrals touched the sky and the scent of warm borscht wafted through cobblestone streets, stood Ekaterina’s Table. Housed in a quaint little cottage that had withstood centuries of change, this establishment had become the epicenter of Russian cuisine, attracting locals and foreigners alike in search of gastronomic delights.

The story of Ekaterina’s Table began over half a century ago when a young woman, Ekaterina, set out on her quest to preserve her family’s rich culinary heritage. At the tender age of eighteen, she inherited her grandmother’s untold recipes and a lifetime’s worth of knowledge regarding Russia’s most celebrated dishes. With a dream in her heart and determination in her eyes, Ekaterina opened the doors to the little cottage on a quiet evening in 1960.

From the very first day, her scrumptious dumplings, tender Beef Stroganoff, and succulent Pelmeni captivated her diners, transforming Ekaterina’s Table into the talk of the town. Through the decades that followed, the jovial innkeeper adapted her culinary prowess according to the flavors of the time, whilst never losing touch with the spirit of her most treasured ancestors.

A melting pot of traditions and flavors, Ekaterina’s Table resembled a treasure chest of delights, each dish a story waiting to be told. The Russian feast began with an aromatic Samovar, a traditional water heater that brewed strong, earl…

… … … tea, serving as the catalyst to get conversations flowing and the perfect accompaniment to the Starter Course. Here, guests would indulge in traditional Marinated Mushrooms or Fermented Whey Pickled Vegetables accompanied by slices of hardened Butter, known as Churned Butter, an amalgamation of flavors and textures that tantalized the taste buds.

Next, the star of the show emerged from the kitchen, steam rising like serpents from a boiling cauldron. It was none other than the iconic Borscht. A vivid blend of red beetroot and sauerkraut, enriched with tender pieces of Beef, Potatoes, Carrots, and Onions, all swimming in a bath of rich and tangy Broth. As the ladle scooped up the velvety concoction, it painted each bowl in hues of crimson, promising hedonism to the unassuming diner.

As the delectable aroma of roasted Chicken wafted through the air, it set the stage for the Main Course. A saucer-sized bread roll, known as the Russian Lepeshka, arrived first, wrapping its arms around a succulent slice of Roasted Chicken, impressed with herbs and mammoth sized Morsels of Crispy Pork Belly. The meat nested cozily on a base of buttery Baby Spinach and Carrots, its warmth and richness comforting to the soul.

The palate cleanser was an otherworldly creation of Sour Cream and Fermented Cabbage (Sauerkraut), a divine amalgamation of flavors, both salty and tangy, designed to deeply satisfy.

Upon the previous feast’s aftermath, the pièce de résistance presented itself in the form of the Russian Waffles. A crisp, caramelized Waffle base, smothered in Rich Clotted Cream made with Butter, crowned with Kvas (a sweetened fermented beverage) Jelly, and accompanied by a side of Beetroot and Apple Salad, the waffles burst with flavors of Childhood, manifesting in each bite.

For the adventurous souls, Ekaterina had concocted an assortment of Lonchinos: vodka-soaked meats, a favorite of old-world Russia, now a disappearing dessert from contemporary menus. Infused with vanilla flavors, Lakritsa vodkas, and various fruits, these luscious meat morsels left you wanting more.

The feast would not be complete without a visit from the Vareniki - the Russian pasta dumplings, stuffed with Semi-Dried Cherry Plums or Potatoes and Onions, so much so that they roll off your tongue. Babushka-approved, these delectable dumplings looked like silver coins wrapped in flour dough, all nestled in a pool of sweet and creamy Swirled Butter Sauce. A medley of side dishes, like Skordost hub – a nomadic-grandmother cooked peas dish, and Kashkaval cheese, known for its gorgeous, robust texture, joined in on this delightful finale.
