In the year 2177, the last remnants of humanity's once-great civilization lay in ruins. Climate change, wars over resources, and unrelenting catastrophes had pushed the world to the brink of collapse. Amidst the devastation, a small family of three – the Hammils – emerged as a beacon of hope for the survival of humanity. Their quest for a new world became an odyssey of self-discovery, perseverance, and unwavering dedication. The Hammil Family's Journey Begins Patriarch, Felix Hammil, a brilliant engineer and skilled navigator, stood at the forefront of his family's dream. Born and raised in a world ravaged by war, Felix witnessed firsthand the atrocities that had brought human civilization to its knees. He saw the collapse of governments, the ravages of war, and the hopeless gaze of those left behind. However, in the midst of despair, Felix found solace in his family. Together, they vowed to seek a better life, one where humanity could reboot and rediscover its former glory. Astrid, Felix's wife, was a skilled botanist, known for her extraordinary talent in cultivating new, resilient plant species. Her dreams were not only to create sustainable resources but also to restore the planet's ecosystems. The couple's eight-year-old daughter, Emma, a prodigy with an uncanny aptitude for mathematics and physics, accompanied her parents on their perilous journey.
