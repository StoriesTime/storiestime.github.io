The Last Human: Chronicles from the Final Frontier


As the last remnants of humanity dwindled, the once vibrant and thriving colonies across the galaxy succumbed to the ravages of time and the relentless onslaught of a deadly, relentless virus that had decimated entire planets, pouring a final stamp of desolation on an already devastated existence. Earth, The Final Resting Place Amidst the desolate ruins of a system intact no more, humanity holed up, preserving the delicate infrastructure that had managed to cling to existence primarily on planet GA-0003, codenamed Nora. It was on this forsaken world, reborn as Nora 2.5 alpha phase three, where The Last Human found herself amidst a glittering spread of abandoned ruins against a dark, and sorrowfully barren sky.
