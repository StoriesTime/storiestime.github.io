The Hero's Quest: A Modern Take on Arthurian Legends


In the bustling metropolis of New Camelot, a city of steel and stone, a legendary tale unfolds. It is a story of one man's journey, a hero's quest, that echoes through the annals of time. His name is Arthur Blackwood, a brilliant and charismatic leader, destined to wield the sword of justice and bring order to a world on the brink of chaos. The Calling of the Ancients Arthur's odyssey begins in the vast, futuristic city, where technology and magic have merged to create a world of wonder and discovery. He is a young Lt. Commander in the New Camelot Defense Force, tasked with maintaining peace and security in the city. However, Arthur's life takes a dramatic turn when he is summoned by an enigmatic figure known only as Merlin, the wise and powerful mentor.

The Design of Destiny: Merlin reveals to Arthur that he is the chosen one, destined to wield the legendary sword of the Ancients, Excalibur II. The sword, forged from the very essence of the Earth, holds the power to vanquish evil and protect the innocent. Riddles of the Past: Merlin shares with Arthur a glimpse of the ancient prophecy, foretelling the rise of a great hero who will bring balance to the universe. As Arthur ponders the significance of his mission, he begins to experience strange and vivid visions, a whispered call to adventure that echoes through his very being. Guardians of the Sword: Before embarking on his perilous journey, Arthur must confront the three mystical guardians of Excalibur II, each possessing a unique aspect of the sword's power.

The Sojourn in Avalon Arthur's quest next takes him to the mystical realm of Avalon, a realm hidden within the very fabric of the city's energy grid. Here, he must navigate the treacherous landscape of data servers, mainframes, and virtual realms to reach the fabled Isle of the Ancients.

Ransom of Time: Arthur must pay the ransom of his heart's desires to unlock the secrets of the ancient systems. Each path and each decision reveals a glimmer of his own true nature. Competing Fates: As Arthur reaches the gates of Avalon, he is confronted by a rival claimant, another Arthur, who seeks to claim Excalibur II for his own. Puzzle of Continence: In the now iconoclastic Oracle house, Arthur is forced to confront the enigmatic System Oracle of Avalon.
