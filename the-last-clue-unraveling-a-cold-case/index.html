Emma Taylor, a 25-year-old artist, had vanished after a night out with friends at a local bar in 1995. Her parents had filed a missing person's report, but as the days turned into weeks and the weeks into months, the police investigation had stalled. They interviewed her friends, recalling the details of that fateful evening - the conversations, the laughter, and the doubt that began to creep in when Emma excused herself to use the restroom, never to return.
