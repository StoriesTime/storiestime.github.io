The Little Mermaid's Wish: A Bedtime Story for Kids

3 min · 562 words

Once upon a time, in the sparkling depths of the ocean, there lived a little mermaid named Marina. She had the most beautiful golden hair and a voice that could enchant anyone who heard it. Marina’s greatest wish was to explore the world above the water, where humans lived.

Every night, Marina would sit on a rock near the surface of the sea and dream of what it would be like to walk on land, to feel the warmth of the sun on her face, and to dance under the moonlight. She longed to see the colorful flowers, hear the laughter of children, and experience the wonders of the human world.

One day, while Marina was swimming near the coral reefs, she spotted a ship sailing above her. Curiosity overwhelmed her, and she couldn’t resist swimming closer to get a better look. As she peered through the crystal-clear water, she saw a handsome prince standing on the ship’s deck. His smile was as bright as the sun, and Marina couldn’t help but fall in love with him at first sight.

As fate would have it, a sudden storm brewed, and the ship was tossed and turned by the raging waves. Marina watched in horror as the prince was thrown overboard and began to sink. Without thinking twice, she swam to his rescue, guiding him to the safety of a nearby island.

When the prince regained consciousness, he found himself lying on the white sandy beach. Marina watched him from a distance, her heart filled with both joy and sadness. She knew that as a mermaid, she could never be with him on land.

Days turned into weeks, and Marina watched the prince slowly recover. She sang to him every night, her voice carrying across the waves, soothing his troubled dreams. The prince became enchanted by the mysterious voice, unable to forget the angelic sound that brought him comfort.

One night, as Marina sang her sweet melodies, a magical sea creature appeared before her. It was a wise old sea witch who had been watching Marina’s selfless act of love. The sea witch knew of Marina’s deepest wish to be with the prince and offered her a deal.

“You may become human, Marina,” the sea witch said, “but in exchange, you must give me your beautiful voice. Without it, you will never be able to sing again.”

Marina hesitated, knowing that her voice was her most precious gift. But her love for the prince was stronger than anything else, so she agreed to the sea witch’s bargain.

In an instant, Marina felt her tail transform into legs, and she stood on the shore as a human for the first time. She looked into the prince’s eyes, and he was captivated by her beauty. However, when Marina tried to speak, no words came out. The sea witch had taken her voice as promised.

Despite not being able to communicate with the prince, Marina stayed by his side, hoping that her love and presence would be enough. She danced with him on the beach, and they laughed together, their hearts connected in a way that words could never express.

Days turned into months, and the prince grew sadder with each passing day. He knew there was something missing, something he couldn’t quite grasp. One night, as the moon shone brightly overhead, Marina found herself standing on the same rock where she used to dream of the human world.

With tears in her eyes, Marina made a wish upon the stars. She wished for the prince’s happiness, even if it meant leaving him forever. As her wish echoed through the night, a miracle happened. Marina’s voice, more beautiful than ever, returned to her.

Overwhelmed with joy, Marina ran back to the prince and sang the most enchanting melody he had ever heard. Tears streamed down his face as he realized that the voice he had been longing for all along belonged to Marina, the mermaid who had saved his life.

Their love for each other grew stronger, and the prince vowed to never leave Marina’s side. Together, they explored the wonders of the human world, hand in hand, creating their own fairy tale.

And so, the little mermaid’s wish came true. Marina’s selfless act of love and her unwavering determination had brought her the happiness she had always dreamed of. The ocean rejoiced, and every night, Marina’s voice could be heard, reminding everyone of the power of love and the beauty of dreams.
