Dreams Alight: Short Stories of Aspiration


Once upon a time, in a quaint little village nestled deep within the heart of a meticulously crafted world, there lived a young man by the name of Eamon. Eamon was as ordinary as they came, performing the menial tasks villagers required to live their lives – tending to the fields as his family had for centuries. Yet, unlike his brethren who admired the comforts of the predictable, he was consumed by distant desires he could barely articulate, thus igniting his inexhaustible curiosity about life beyond the confines of his mundane routine.

Eamon had dreams so grand that they ignited in his chest, sparked fire in his thoughts, and danced in the depths of his mind each night. Dreams of dilating vistas and unimaginable creatures, dreams of skillful artistry and people who could pioneer his world with their boundless wisdom. In his reveries, he found purpose and direction unattainable in reality.

Night after night Eamon visited these dreams, they burned into his consciousness as though some unseen hand had dipped him in dreams alight. Yet when he awoke, reality weighed upon him like a depressurized world. For all of the enchantment that dreams held, Eamon was confined by the wisdom of his forefathers, the “god-tier” decree from his elders.

“Do not venture far, for the unknown may swallow you whole.” His parents lived by this book, their eyes dragged from happening around them and singed with caution, keeping hold of their traditions.

In the heart of his despair, Eamon found an invitation for an ancient gathering, held annually where villagers convened to venerate the spirits of their ancestors, a time to cast off the year’s misery and mend their wooden statues. Surrounded by the warm laughter of his peers and the intense piety of his elders, Eamon grasped this as his ultimate opportunity.

His eyes sparkled with mischief as he wagered to douse the village with an unexplored experience, concocting a daring plan to light the meeting on fire, metaphorically, of course. Eamon would use his tale to inspire his defiant dreams onto his community who had never tasted newfound horizons.

In the dead of the night’s encompassing mystique, as the spirits of his ancestors bore the weight of Eamon’s responsibility, his plan began to unfurl. He would dazzle his village with theories of entire multiverses – secrets of the parallel dimensions, keys to otherworldly realms of dreams, the boundless knowledge they could soak up from the stars above, and the oceans of grand dreams that would enthrall his village, reinforcing the allure of elevation he so craved.

For days, nights, and the wide baskets of unending intermissions, he read at breakneck speed. As his eyes exhausted their source of focus, the letters shaping words with the force of dreams alight seemed to confide in Eamon that he’d be the first to imbibe such grand universes.

On the eve of the annual gathering, Eamon’s pulse raced to the clip of the moon against the canvas sky, the magnificent dreams he had been unknowingly dreaming for eons now filled his thoughts with a thick, shimmering gold. His plan was flawless, one final embrace of courage kept his vision bubbling beneath the surface.

The day dawned, and as the sun played a wistful melody of gold on the earth before retreating to slumber, the village prepared in their humblest reverence. Eamon beheld his brethren in their quiet seclusion, observing the colors of their eyes, flecked with admiration and gratitude, their souls a mere waiting chasm.

With the village’s hopeful expectation tattooed in his heart, Eamon rose to share the dreams alight he’d discovered. Yet, he came face to face with a seemingly clinging uncertainty. His hands trembling, Eamon’s eyes flickered nervously as the silent assembly watched, their faces glowing with a weak smile and hopeful anticipation.

By his ancestors’ fires, Eamon had found a hundred thousand dreams within the stars, the trees, the air evaporating with the whisper of the moon. What he discovered was that dreams alight would only burn with the likes of those ready to fan the flames of the heart; for this is where dreams find their shape and their fate.

To embrace that notion, Eamon peered deep within his brethren, knowing the ancient wisdom inscribed on their faces, and the boundless dreams locked within their hearts. Eamon made a quiet, firm decision: to share the beauty contained in the tried-and-true wisdom of their ancestors, breathing new life into the teachings that would guide and nurture them.

Eamon’s eyes twinkled under the moon’s gaze as he slowly unfurled the secrets of their forefathers’ dreams. And as he did, the villege embraced these newly fanned flames, captivated by the wisdom gifted from the heavens. And by some shared, ancient virtue, the village itself destined their children to wonders and dreams otherwise unimaginable.

The dreams alight emerged as carriers of Eamon’s passionate allure, for he was their key. The breathless realization of the dreams bound within the heart of countless brethren rippling in harmony with his thoughts - they could thrive, transcendent of the elders’ wishes to keep the traditions anchored to the Earth’s pulse.
