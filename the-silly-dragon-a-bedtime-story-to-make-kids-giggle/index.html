The Silly Dragon: A Bedtime Story to Make Kids Giggle

3 min · 562 words

Once upon a time, in a land far, far away, there lived a dragon named Doodle. Now, Doodle was not your ordinary fire-breathing, fearsome dragon. No, Doodle was a silly dragon, always getting into trouble and making everyone around him giggle.

Doodle had bright blue scales that shimmered in the sunlight, and a long, curly tail that he loved to wag like a dog. His wings were small and fluffy, making it hard for him to fly properly. But that didn’t stop him from trying.

Every day, Doodle would wake up with a mischievous sparkle in his eyes. He would stretch his wings and yawn, ready for a day full of fun and laughter. But before he could start his adventures, he had one important task to complete - brushing his teeth!

You see, dragons have big, sharp teeth, and it’s essential for them to take care of them. Doodle would grab his toothbrush, which was as big as his tail, and squeeze a dollop of toothpaste onto it. But instead of using just a little bit, Doodle would cover his toothbrush with toothpaste from top to bottom, making a big, frothy mess in his mouth.

With toothpaste dripping from his mouth, Doodle would then start brushing his teeth. But instead of using gentle, circular motions, he would brush his teeth with wild, zig-zag strokes, causing toothpaste to fly everywhere. It would splatter on the walls, the mirror, and even on Doodle’s face.

Once he was done brushing, Doodle would rinse his mouth with water, but he wouldn’t stop there. Oh no! Doodle loved to blow bubbles with the water in his mouth. He would gargle and swish the water around, creating a symphony of bubbles that floated in the air. Sometimes, the bubbles would pop, and Doodle would laugh so hard that he would accidentally spit water all over the bathroom floor.

After his toothbrushing adventure, Doodle would head out into the village, where he would spread laughter wherever he went. He would walk up to the villagers and tell them silly jokes that made them chuckle. His favorite joke was, “Why did the chicken go to the seashore? Because it wanted to see the chicken of the sea!”

The villagers would always laugh and pat Doodle on the head, grateful for the joy he brought to their lives. But Doodle’s mischief didn’t stop there. He loved to pull pranks too. One time, he tied all the villagers’ shoelaces together, so when they tried to walk, they stumbled and fell in a heap. Another time, he hid behind trees and bushes, tickling the villagers’ noses with a feather, making them sneeze uncontrollably.

But even though Doodle loved to play pranks, he was always careful not to hurt anyone’s feelings. He knew the importance of laughter, but he also knew the importance of kindness. So, whenever someone didn’t find his pranks funny, he would apologize and offer them a silly dance to make up for it. And most of the time, his silly dances would turn their frowns upside down.

As the sun began to set, Doodle would fly back to his cozy cave, where his bed was made of fluffy clouds. He would curl up, his tail wrapped around him like a blanket, and drift off to sleep with a smile on his face, knowing that he had brought joy and laughter to the world.

And so, every night, children from all over the land would beg their parents to tell them the story of the silly dragon named Doodle. They would giggle and laugh, imagining themselves brushing their teeth with toothpaste splattering everywhere, or tickling their friends’ noses with a feather.

The story of Doodle, the silly dragon, reminded everyone that laughter is a powerful gift that can bring people together, even in the most unexpected ways. And as the children drifted off to sleep with smiles on their faces, they knew that Doodle’s silly antics would always bring them joy and make their dreams a little brighter.

So, the next time you see a dragon, remember Doodle, the silly dragon who made everyone giggle. And who knows, maybe you’ll find a little bit of Doodle’s silliness within you, ready to spread laughter wherever you go.
