Tomorrow's Villain: A Prequel of Evil Unfolding


Once upon a time, in a village nestled between lush green mountains, bloomed a peculiar and enigmatic rose bush. The villagers had admired the roses for generations, and they were the talk of every neighborhood gathering. But little did they know that one of these roses possessed a malevolent presence, secretly plotting the downfall of its beautiful abode.

The rose, named Thorns of Twilight, wasn’t particularly unique in appearance. It was neither the most vibrant nor the most fragrant among its kin, but it possessed a hidden quality, a sinister charm that drew people into its twisted embrace. The villagers used to ignore it, dismissing it as plain and mundane, unaware that darker forces lay nestled in its velvety petals.

Our story begins with Nari, a curious and adventurous young lass, who always dreamt of uncovering the secret wonders of their village. One sunny afternoon, Nari, in her enthusiasm, decided to pluck a rose from Thorns of Twilight, longing to unravel its hidden magic. As she drew closer, a foreboding chill ran down her spine, but her curiosity overpowered her fear, and she plucked the rose with nimble fingers.

Instantly, the rose released a mesmerizing aroma, capturing Nari’s senses and rendering her helpless in its clutches. The more she tried to wriggle free, the stronger the rose’s charm became, pulling her into its winding vines. Nari woke up the next day in a dense forest far away from her beloved village, still grasping the thorny rose in her hand.

Thorns of Twilight was not alone, and it had sent out seedlings to different parts of the world, waiting patiently for an opportune moment to establish its sinister influence. One seedling took root in a bustling metropolis, where an ambitious politician named Victor always wished to see his city flourish.

Victor’s heart whirled with desires of power and control. He started penning down a grand plan, of ruling his city with an iron fist, subduing any form of protest or dissent with brute force. The seedling, sensing its opportunity, bound itself to Victor’s mind, whispering dreams of red power, transforming his ambitions into darker schemes. Thorns of Twilight had found its ally in its bid to shake the foundations of their world.

As Victor rose to power, the sinister plot of Thorns of Twilight unraveled. The village, not realizing the truth behind the strange malaise that had overtaken the thriving community, began to deteriorate. Families turned against one another. The beloved bountiful farmyards lay barren. The colors of happiness and laughter melted away into the shadows of despair.

One day, Nari, battered and bruised from her own struggles to understand the unexplained chaos and her inexplicable connection to the mysterious rose, finally pieced together the link between Thorns of Twilight and the corrupted state of her dear village. She knew she must take action to save her beloved people.

With a newfound clarity, Nari ventured into the heart of the city, preparing herself to face their future villain, Victor. Their fateful encounter revealed the devious truths behind Victor’s rapid ascent, as Nari sought to transform their reality and restore hope to their once-harmonious home.

Swords clashed as Nari fought tooth and nail against Victor’s henchmen. The young lass, armed only with her wits and heart’s pure purpose, advanced toward the ambitious politician, ready to confront the rising power of Thorns of Twilight and save her village from its impending evil.

As Nari fought to free her world from its sinister curse, she reaffirmed her unbreakable resilience and bond with her cherished village, taking the first steps in overcoming the fearsome reign of Tomorrow’s Villain.
