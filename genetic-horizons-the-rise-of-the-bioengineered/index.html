The city of New Eden, London, was home to the most advanced genetic research facility in the world. Dr. Rachel Kim, a renowned geneticist, had dedicated her life to pushing the boundaries of human enhancement. Her laboratory was a marvel of modern technology, filled with rows of humming incubators and state-of-the-art equipment. Rachel's latest project was the most ambitious yet: a gene-edited human embryo, code-named "Elysium." Elysium was designed to possess the pinnacle of human intelligence, predicted to surpass even the greatest minds in history. The parents of the embryo, Valentina and Liam, were eager to see their child become the next step in human evolution.
