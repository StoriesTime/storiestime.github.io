The elderly were treated with reverence and respect, their experiences and wisdom cherished and passed down through generations. Children grew up in an environment that was both nurturing and intellectually stimulating, encouraged to explore their passions and pursue their dreams without constraints. The decision to create the city of Elyria was made by a coalition of visionary leaders who, after centuries of conflict and strife, had come to realize the need for a radical departure from the conventional norms. They spent decades searching for a solution, pouring over the collective knowledge of humanity, and applying innovative thinking to create a new way of living. The results of their tireless efforts were nothing short of miraculous. This is where the leaders of Elyria reside, directing the affairs of the city with a calm and deliberate hand.
