As she approached the perimeter fence, Aria donned a disguise, her eyes gleaming with determination. A sleek black wig, a new identity, and a prison-issue jumpsuit that reeked of despair. She blended in seamlessly among the other inmates, her eyes never leaving the fence as she absorbed every detail. The first test came in the form of a gruff guard, his eyes narrowing as he scrutinized her. Aria flashed a sheepish grin, her eyes sparkling with a hint of mischief. "Hey, girl, where you headed?" he asked, his tone gruff but not unfriendly. "In here, for the food. And maybe a new name, if you know what I mean, huh?" Aria smiled, her Southern drawl dripping with charm. The guard chuckled, waving her through. "Welcome to Oakwood, sweetheart. Just watch yourself in there. It ain't no picnic."
