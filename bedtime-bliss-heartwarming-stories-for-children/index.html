Bedtime Bliss: Heartwarming Stories for Children

3 min · 623 words

Little Li, a very imaginative child, loved to dream up beautiful stories in the dark of night. His room was always lit up by the window, with the night sky outside being so vast and dark.

有一天，小李发现，他在睡梦中总是看到一个美丽的花园。花园中有各种颜色的花朵，各种形状的树木，各种音色的鸟儿。小李非常想知道，这个花园是谁的？为什么他总是梦见这个花园？

One day, Little Li discovered that he always saw a beautiful garden in his dreams. The garden had flowers of various colors, trees of various shapes, and birds of various sounds. Little Li was very curious to know who the owner of the garden was and why he always dreamed of it.

他开始调查这个花园的秘密。他问了许多人，但是没有人知道这个花园的秘密。小李感到非常沮丧，但是他并没有放弃。他继续调查，直到有一天，他发现了一个小小的门。这个门是隐藏在花园的最深处。

He began to investigate the secret of the garden. He asked many people, but no one knew the secret of the garden. Little Li felt very disappointed, but he did not give up. He continued to investigate until one day, he found a small door. The door was hidden in the deepest part of the garden.

小李推开了门，发现是一个小小的房子。房子里有一位老人，他的脸上covered by a layer of wrinkles，眼睛非常温柔。老人看到小李后，问他：“你是谁？为什么来找我？”

Little Li pushed open the door and found a small house. Inside the house was an old man, whose face was covered by a layer of wrinkles, with very gentle eyes. The old man saw Little Li and asked him, “Who are you? Why have you come to find me?”

小李告诉老人，他是小李，是一个梦想家。他梦见这个花园，非常想知道这个花园是谁的。老人听了小李的故事后，微微一笑。他说：“我是这个花园的主人，我叫梦想家。这个花园是我的梦想。”

Little Li told the old man that he was Little Li, a dreamer. He dreamed of this garden and very much wanted to know who the owner of the garden was. The old man listened to Little Li’s story and smiled slightly. He said, “I am the owner of this garden, and I am called Dreamer. This garden is my dream.”

小李感到非常惊奇。他问梦想家，为什么他总是梦见这个花园？梦想家回答说：“我想让你知道，这个花园是我的梦想，是我在梦中创造的。你是一个梦想家，我想让你知道梦想的价值。”

Little Li felt very surprised. He asked Dreamer why he always dreamed of this garden. Dreamer replied, “I want you to know that this garden is my dream, which I created in my dreams. You are a dreamer, and I want you to know the value of dreams.”

小李感到非常感动。他问梦想家，如何才能梦见这个花园？梦想家回答说：“你需要有一个好心和好梦想。你需要每天晚上睡前，想象你梦见的花园。然后，你将能够梦见这个花园。”

Little Li felt very moved. He asked Dreamer how he could dream of this garden. Dreamer replied, “You need to have a kind heart and good dreams. You need to imagine your dream garden every night before going to sleep. Then, you will be able to dream of this garden.”

小李非常高兴。他决定每天晚上睡前，想象这个花园。他做到了，并且梦见了这个花园。他感到非常高兴，因为他终于能够梦见这个花园了。

Little Li was very happy. He decided to imagine this garden every night before going to sleep. He did so, and he dreamed of this garden. He felt very happy because he finally could dream of this garden.
