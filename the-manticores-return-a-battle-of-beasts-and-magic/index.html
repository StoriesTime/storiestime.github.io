Epilogue: A New Dawn

The days after the Manticore’s defeat were quiet, but the world had changed. Alaric and his companions were hailed as heroes, their names forever etched into the annals of history. But Alaric knew the true battle was not over. Dark forces still lingered, hidden in the shadows, waiting for the right moment to strike.

He had seen the power of magic, and he knew that it could either be a weapon of salvation or destruction. His journey was far from finished, and the true test of his strength—his courage—had yet to come.

As he looked out over the land, Alaric made a silent vow to protect the realm, no matter the cost. For the Manticore’s return had only been the beginning, and the battle of beasts and magic was a war that would last for generations to come.
