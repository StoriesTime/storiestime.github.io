Veilwalker: One Step from the Paranormal


A soft veil of mist hung heavy in the old Oakwood village, so dense and all-consuming that no one could see farther than the tip of their nose. Young Elara had wandered too deep into the woods, where even the ancient trees whispered with the knowledge of unknown worlds. Time slowed as she stumbled upon an old stone archway nestled between the gnarled roots of ancient oaks. A flickering glimpse of some other realm beyond pierced her vision, and in an instant, the line between Veilwalker and the paranormal blurred.

Elara heard the distorted voice of an old crone calling her name, urging her to cross. The knowing warmth of the other side was no defense against the inescapable pull of those echoing whispers. Without a second thought, she reached out her hand and felt the cold stone under her fingers, her feet icing as they crossed the threshold into a world utterly unexplored.

Farther still, the crimson haze coalesced into the form of Veilwalkers: a shadow-covered band of spectral warriors. The shadows lifted from their forms at Elara’s touch, revealing eyes that glowed like embers, their otherworldly beauty hauntingly captivating.

Through their time-hallowed eyes, Elara glimpsed into a world of mysteries and untold history – a world of glimpses and occluded truths. These spectral sentinels had guarded Oakwood her entire life and were now extended a unique invitation to take her in, to share stories and secrets of that crumbling archway.
