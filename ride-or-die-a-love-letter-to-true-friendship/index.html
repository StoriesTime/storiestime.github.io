Ride or Die: A Love Letter to True Friendship


Our story starts at the bottom of a mountain, a dusty backpacker’s haven hidden amongst rugged peaks. Lila, with her fiery red hair and wild emerald eyes, sat in a corner booth, nursing a drink while watching the world go by. She was the embodiment of untamed spirit, averaging a new adventure every season. Next to her sat Jamie, a quiet, bookish sort, his awkward large frame taking up more space than either of them would’ve liked in the dimly lit bar.

In the several years they’ve known each other, Lila and Jamie had nothing in common, but who needed a mutual interest when they had each other? They bonded over their shared passion for wanderlust, gossiped about obscure folk tales, and cheered one another on during their own, separate escapades. They were ride or die - two peas in a pod, friends that were more like family.

It was the quiet before the storm and the two hadn’t seen each other in months. Lila stood up abruptly and brushed past the woven strands of her tangled hair while her green eyes dared Jamie to keep up. He never needed any more encouragement, following in her wake without reservation. That was the essence of their friendship; it was organic, wholly messy, and unquestionably solid.

Chapter 1: The Twinning Ceremony

But the friends’ reunion took an unexpected turn. Hovering in the shadows of the majestic mountain stood an ancient portal. Rumor had it, an age-old ceremony would bind the first souls to cross the threshold, forging a bond so close that their lives would be intertwined.

“We should go through it,” Lila proposed dramatically. She propped her hands on her hips and wiggled her eyebrows, already in ardent agreement.

Despite the implausible embroidery of their lives, Jamie was ruled by logic. “Are you out of your mind?” If he could’ve physically marched her out of the situation, he would’ve. But there was admiration in his voice.

Their friendship had always danced on the edge of belief and madness. Jumping into a mystical portal wouldn’t be out of character.

With a mutual nod that spoke their acceptance, Lila charged forward. Jamie hesitated for just a moment, feeling the ground falter beneath him, but he couldn’t resist the pull of their ride or die companionship. He caught up beside Lila and together they stepped through the archway of the mysterious portal. The world shimmered and transformed.

They found themselves on the edge of a vast desert, a kaleidoscope of dunes stretching out before them. The sun hung low in the sky, painting the horizon in fire as if they had stepped out of an adventure novel.

As the days turned into weeks, the duo traversed the sprawling unknown, encountering mythical creatures and ancient civilizations. But no challenge fazed them, for they were united, stronger in their bond than ever before. Conflicts dissipated as they disarmingly defied the odds. Whispers of their extraordinary tale became a legend, persisting through the sands of time.

Just as the horizon welcomed twilight, the time came for Lila and Jamie to make their way back home. The portal had pushed them to their limits, but they faced these trials together - ride or die. A shimmering archway appeared before them, beckoning them to retrace their steps.

Upon returning to their world, Lila and Jamie discovered that they were changed - not by a supernatural ceremony, but by their journey. Their rich friendship only deepened with their shared experiences, giving them an unbreakable connection.

With a newfound introspective prism, they approached their different yet complimentary existences. As Lila reveled in her carefree adventures, Jamie found solace in his newfound appreciation of their bond. Reflecting on their journey, they both realized that adventures, after all, should come with great companionship.

The tale of their wild ride became interwoven into the cosmic fabric, waiting to be discovered by those with the heart and courage to search for it. It all started with a portal, an extraordinary adventure, but what truly mattered was the epitome of Lila and Jamie’s bond – ride or die.
