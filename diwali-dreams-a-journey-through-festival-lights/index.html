Diwali Dreams: A Journey Through Festival Lights


As the sun sets over the bustling streets of Mumbai, the air is filled with the sweet scent of diyas, beckoning in the festival of lights - Diwali. It's a time of great joy and celebration, a delight for the senses. The streets are alive with the sound of laughter, the sparkle of lights, and the sound of whispered prayers. Today, I am on a special mission to recreate the iconic Diwali dream experience, exploring the essence of the festival that has been illuminating India for centuries. I set out early in the morning, eager to experience the magic of Diwali. Morning of Bright Lights My journey begins at Fatehpur Sikri, a small town near Agra, famous for its magnificent Jamuna Daiwalla temple, where the grandeur of Diwali is on full display. As I enter the temple, I am immediately engulfed by the vibrant aura of twinkling diyas and glittering candles. The devotees have spent the night in the temple, praying and chanting, and the atmosphere is electrified with devotion. As we walk through the streets of the town, the morning light brings to life the firework displays set off the night before. The threadbare rooftops are decorated with sparkling diyas and curtains of light playfully dancing in the sun. I feel as if I have walked into a kaleidoscope of colors, with each shop and street corner radiating pure joy. A Symphony of Lights Next, I head to the iconic Delhi market, Chandi Chowk. With my friend Sunita, a local resident, I walk through the crowded streets, weaving through intricately decorated streets and enroute exploring bustling lanes and markets. Every single landmark and shop-worn building has been beautifully decorated, filled with mythological stories and intricate patterns. You can experience the perfect flavor of Diwali in the ancient structures incorporating the very essence of mythology.
