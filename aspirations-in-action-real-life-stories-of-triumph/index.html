Aspirations in Action: Real-Life Stories of Triumph


Sarah was no ordinary 16-year-old girl. While her peers were content with social media and texting their friends, Sarah had a different aspiration - to become an engineer. Growing up in a small town in India, she was aware of the numerous opportunities that lay beyond her rural village. Ever since she was a child, she had been fascinated by the intricate working of machines and gadgets. She spent countless hours asking her father, a mechanic, to explain the details of how things worked. Her curiosity and interest in science and math led her to pursue engineering as her career goal. As she entered into high school, Sarah began to feel the weight of societal expectations. Her parents and family members urged her to focus on medicine or commerce, considering engineering to be an unattainable pursuit. Undeterred, Sarah read extensively on the subject, attended workshops, and sought guidance from teachers who shared her passion. She spent long hours on self-study, transforming stems from simple machines to complex mechanical devices in her mind's eye. However, the journey was not an easy one. Without support from family and friends, every challenge became insurmountable. There were times when she thought of quitting, feeling isolated and dejected. Nevertheless, she walked a precarious tightrope between her determination to follow her dreams and a future filled with uncertainty. For every step forward, there were many set-backs, but Sarah persevered. Interaction with her uncle, a civil engineer in the city, reignited her ambitions. He offered her words of encouragement, questioned the reluctance to disrelish passion for engineering. There came a time when an internship opportunity beckoned, which added further momentum to her determination. Her bosses, a few senior engineers, noticed her courage, dependability and allowed her access to projects that pushed her skills. As news of her exploits spread, whispers of `gifted' teenager reached her locality, shaking the reluctant minds and emotional forest of everyone who knew her. Prior to everyone's surprise and hesitation, Sarah landed a place in an elite engineering college, much to the great agony and sullenness of a section of villagers. Shaping the Lines of a Bright Future Within the walls of her new college, Sarah began to break through every impediment. With each passing semester, her convictions solidified, painting an unseen picture of an undeniable success story. Despite never landing top grades in school, her sincerity, equity of spirit, and philanthropy earned the trust of faculty and students alike. Many colleagues admired her confrontational arguments, encouragement which endeared them to navigate the building block of her mentor-ship.
