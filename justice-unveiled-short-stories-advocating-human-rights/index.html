Justice Unveiled: Short Stories Advocating Human Rights


It was a lesser-known secret that Almira’s town had more than its fair share of inexplicable phenomena. Some would attribute this to the residents, others to the land itself. Within the Circle’s borders, Almira witnessed things that most people could only read about in books or hear whispered about late at night. Still, every resident knew that keeping the town’s mysterious attributes concealed was a necessity, for if word got out, it would only attract unwanted attention.

Almira deeply believed in the importance of concealing their world’s secrets. As the justice department’s district collective conscience, the onus had been placed upon her to remain steadfast in her convictions. A rank held by her family for generations, Almira was not taken lightly in her role. Her sacred duty was to ensure that no one, regardless of their impressive feats or petty transgressions, went unseen.

One evening, Almira was approached by the cantankerous groundskeeper, Mr. Bumblebree. He had been performing his duties faithfully for decades and still held the icy heart of every villager who crossed his path.

“Betsy Hettler had her garden strawberries stolen,” Bumblebree complained, trying to catch his breath. “Something must be done about this.”

Almira felt the familiar surge of responsibility flooding her veins. “I’ll look into it,” she stated resolutely, fully knowing that the perpetrator would face the swift hand of justice.

For the rest of the day, Almira trailed after trail after lead, all of which dissipated without revealing a single suspect in the case of the pilfered strawberries. When her father took notice of her frustration, he reminded Almira of the town’s old code, an unspoken phrase that enveloped every resident within the Circle. “In light of what lies hidden, no stone may be left unturned.”

Her father’s words jolted Almira back to a forgotten text passed down from generation to generation. It was a brief passage referencing “The one who walks among shadows” – a mysterious figure notorious for cloaking themselves in the darkness and thus mapping the Circle’s secrets. It was said that their shadowy existence had kept the peace for centuries, subtly guiding those in authority when no one else could see the path.

Accustomed to following instructions, Almira summoned the strength to search for the elusive figure. Eventually, she made a startling discovery – the person known as the one who walks among shadows was none other than Betsy Hettler.

The future seemed uncertain as Almira grappled with the knowledge of Betsy’s true identity. Until this point, Betsy had been considered soft-spoken and innocent, albeit a tad naive. This new revelation cast a monumental shadow over Almira’s once-unwavering belief that justice prevailed within the Circle’s confines.

Almira knew she had to confront Betsy. Tearful, hesitant, and full of apologies, Betsy confessed her secret to Almira. She explained that her shadowy role had been passed down through generations of women in her family, fueled by a hidden strength that neither Bumblebree nor Almira’s family could tap into. The responsibility of her role weighed heavily on Betsy, but she could not escape the shady life she had inherited. It was this duality that created the perfect cover for her secret identity.

The truth was undeniable: Betsy, the one who walks among shadows, was both a thief and the silent protector of the Circle. As she handed over the strategically stolen strawberries, Betsy made a heartfelt plea to Almira, begging for her understanding.

In that moment, Almira saw a glimpse of the comprehensive view of justice that had steeped itself, unnoticeably, into the core of the Circle’s people. It was inevitable that in a world of shadows, an equally shaded form of justice had been created to maintain balance.

Almira’s duty was clear – she would need to rewrite her own path. The justice department needed to acknowledge Betsy’s blood-swept role and incorporate it into the Circle’s governing structure. Slowly, her understanding began to unravel the secretive web in which the Circle had been concealed.

Our story concludes with Almira’s next steps. It’s a story of justice unveiled, of secrets unwrapped and entwined in the ever-spinning cycle of life. The delicate dance between what we know and what we don’t know is a spectrum, one that challenges us to believe that even the darkest corners can contain their own form of light.

By embracing the unknown, Almira opened the door to understanding that spans the spectrum of human rights – a story that transcends happenstance and colonial manacles. Our ability to find the justice that lies concealed demands that we continuously question our beliefs and embrace the hidden mysteries that exist within all of us.

If we can learn to stand beside one another in the darkness, we might just step into the light together. Will you join us? The journey awaits.
