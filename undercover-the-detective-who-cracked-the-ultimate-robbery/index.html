His latest assignment placed him in the midst of a high-stakes heist, one that had sent shockwaves through the streets of New York City. The notorious "Nightshade" gang, infamous for their brazen robberies and knack for evading capture, had pulled off the impossible – stealing a priceless cache of priceless artifacts from a heavily guarded museum. The police had been baffled by the gang's modus operandi: meticulous planning, precise execution, and an uncanny ability to leave no trail. They had been expecting a replication of their previous tactics, but Nightshade had upped the ante.
