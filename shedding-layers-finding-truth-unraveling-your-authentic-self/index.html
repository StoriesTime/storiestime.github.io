Shedding Layers, Finding Truth: Unraveling Your Authentic Self


When Lila started her yoga practice five years ago, she never imagined she’d end up delving into the deepest layers of her being. As she inhaled deeply, she noticed the palpable energy vibrating in the room. Her instructor’s voice, soft yet steady, filled the space.

“Today, we’re going to focus on shedding layers, finding truth, and unearthing your most authentic self. Are you ready for the journey?”

Lila nodded, a mixture of excitement and apprehension burning in her chest. She had always felt like she was playing a role in life, wearing a mask to conceal her true identity. She wanted to shed those translucent layers—one by one—so she could finally know herself fully.

As the sun peeked through the floor-to-ceiling windows, casting a warm glow over the room, Lila closed her eyes. The instructor began guiding them through a series of deep breathing exercises. She reminded them to focus on their breath, an anchor for the practice to ground them.

“Breathe in, let it fill your lungs and radiate throughout every cell in your body. And as you exhale, let go of any lingering doubts, inhibitions, or fears.”

Lila’s mind wandered as she focused on her breath. She thought back to her childhood days, filled with laughter and innocent dreams. But as time passed, responsibilities, expectations, and societal pressures layered upon her youthful spirit, forming a thick, impenetrable veil.

She recalled her first day at university—the endless choices, the pressure to conform. She majored in psychology, intending to help those lost in the maze of life. However, upon graduating, she identified her own need for guidance—a journey she was only just beginning.

With each inhale and exhale, Lila felt the layers unravel slightly. Her thoughts became clearer. She realized how much these layers had once protected her and nourished her ego. But they also stifled her spirit, making her glimpse her authentic self only in fleeting moments of vulnerability.

As the class continued, they transitioned into gentle, twisting poses. Lila focused on lengthening her spine and weaving together opposing energy pathways. Her instructor coached her each step of the way.

“Reach your left hand towards the sky, and your right foot towards your left hand. Feel the energy flow as you find your center.”

As she slithered and twisted into her cocoon, Lila felt like she was physically working through her emotional baggage. Layer by layer, she began to understand the importance of embracing her authentic self—the pure, unaffected Lila.

Gradually, they relaxed into Savasana, the final resting position that allows the body to absorb the philosophical musings of their practice. Her instructor’s voice echoed: “And now, breathe deeply and allow any remaining layers or resistance to dissolve. You are safe here, safe in your truth.”

Lila tried not to clench her fists too tightly as old feelings resurfaced—shame, fear, anger—pooling at the surface. One by one, she acknowledged them, letting them seep away as her exhale fanned the embers of her soul.

When the practice concluded, Lila remained motionless for a while, embracing the silence that followed. Suddenly, she felt a profound serenity, a gentle embrace from within. She finally understood that her authentic self was not some distant, enigmatic siren. Instead, it had been there all along—hidden beneath the layers of societal expectations and self-doubt.

As the weeks went by, Lila incorporated many of the lessons from her practice into her daily routine. She learned to pause, take a deep breath, and listen to the voice that whispered the gentle truth within her heart.

In no time, she began feeling a sense of familiarity with her inner self. A newfound strength emerged, making her more confident in her decisions and enabling her to shed the personas she had been imitating like old, worn-out costumes.

She cultivated authentic relationships, allowing her heart to be vulnerable and open. She pursued her passions without seeking validation from the outside world. And as her layers continued to unravel, she realized she had always been a work in progress. Life promised to be an eternal unraveling, and that was perfectly okay.

With each passing day, Lila held onto that deep, humbling sense of vulnerability she found on her yoga mat. She cherished the revelations from her practice and realized that now, more than ever, she was finding her truth.

An authentic self-embrace.
