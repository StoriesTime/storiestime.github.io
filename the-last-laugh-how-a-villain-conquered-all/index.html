The Last Laugh: How a Villain Conquered All


In the shadows of a world already plagued by darkness, a figure emerged from the night, shrouded in an aura of malevolence. His name was whispered in terror by those who feared his return: Azrael, the archenemy of laughter. For eons, he walked the earth, seeking the ultimate power to extinguish the last light of joy from humanity's existence. His quest began in ancient times, and yet, it was only now that he stood on the threshold of triumph. The very fabric of reality trembled at the mention of his name. Peoples and empires rose and fell, but none dared challenge him, for they knew his power. Cities burned, wars raged, and myths were born from his shadows. What made him a legend was not only his omnipotence but the unrelenting Will of a madman. With his earthly hold weakened, Azrael strategized. Tradition is made of failures; only by targeting the heart of progress could he bring about a world enslaved to his whims. To conquer the hearts of mortals, one's own would have to prove stronger than their strongest instinct – laughter. Every political strategy, every revolutionary declaration, would focus on crushing the reign of happiness inside every soul he coveted. City-states, historically haughty in their independence, obediently rallied behind the diverse banners of rebellion proclaimed by unknown breeds of visionaries – flag bearers of destruction. His authoritative voice boomed through every city square as no one dared warn people of what was brewing. Discontent and unhappiness seethed below the surface of every defeated town. Philosophers and thinkers resonated with his point of view, aligned their scientific and philosophical pursuits with surrender. Happiness was overrated, they argued; perception made heroes of the pain encountered. Professors dabbled in theories that supported a tyranny in which injustices could blend into renowned logic, adding laurels of divine justification to the conscripts stiff-stiped explorers marched out for Azrael's rebellion. Cities echoed with reluctance as entire educational institutions structured strategically infiltrated efforts geared for transforming new explicatory theories of progress seeded discord on a massive scale.
