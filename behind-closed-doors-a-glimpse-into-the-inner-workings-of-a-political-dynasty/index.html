Behind Closed Doors: A Glimpse into the Inner workings of a Political Dynasty


Digby Porter, my old friend, was sacked from his job as a journalist. For some reason, he seemed to always be on the receiving end of the powerful when they lost their tempers. He had crossed paths with the influential Halifax family. You see, they owned vast estates in Yorkshire and had a long history of politicians dating back three generations. They were untouchable, mostly due to their wealth and connections worldwide.

However, as Digby’s curiosity got the better of him, he began to suspect there was more to their power than met the eye. When a mysterious package arrived at his doorstep - consisting of a series of cryptic letters - Digby realized the Halifaxes had a dark and troubled past.

This story starts with the last will and testament of Sir Leicester Halifax in 1963.

Dearest descendants, As I write this, I am at the edges of my time. I am writing this to you, my dear children, my lovely wife and my chosen grandson, Robert. When I am gone, dig beneath the rose garden and you will find a statue of Queen Elizabeth I’s ruff proudly bearing a blue and white scar. Underneath it, you will unveil a hidden chamber which contains my true legacy.

The cryptic message should have been a warning but it was not heeded. Twenty years after Sir Leicester’s death, Andrew Halifax, the clan’s spiritual leader, decided to follow the riddles left by his grandfather. By digging beneath the rose garden, he discovered the hidden chamber containing the true legacy of the Halifax family - a safe full of gold, silver, and precious jewels.

Consequently, the clan’s debt was eradicated. The so-called ‘Golden Legacy’ yielded an overwhelming fortune, securing their status as the most affluent noble family in Yorkshire. The box also contained a more sinister surprise - a locket with a miniature portrait, unvarnished by time. The portrait was perfect, save for one strange detail: it depicted a dark robed figure standing behind the queen. This was a mystery in and of itself.

After years of hiding, Andrew found himself corrupted by the vastness of the fortune. The once spiritual leader of the Halifaxes led the family into decadence, charades, and falsity. The ruff immediately became an iconic symbol of vanity and superficiality.

From that day until now, the young servant Catherine silently watched as the family decayed around her. She remembered the whispers of Henry, the previous butler, about a night 50 years ago, when the statue was “reset”. This ignited a burning disgust within Catherine, and she was determined to uncover the truth behind this dark legacy.

With a fresh fire and renewed energy, Digby began investigating Catherine’s theory - but, where does one even start? And who wants to know the answer? The truth behind the Golden Legacy that held the family together was now the family’s secret mission to destroy the queen. It was a shocking reality none could escape.

Exhausted by years of resentment and paranoia, our inquiries led us to the dark corners of history. It wasn’t until we decoded the microdot message within the locket that we found the most startling truth: Queen Elizabeth’s cloak was the missing piece in the bloodline tale hidden by the ruff. This information was meant to be shared among a select few and kept within a protected space.

Our search led us across the world, decoding cryptic messages and retracing the steps of a secret society. In the end, we learned that the real power of the Halifax family was not in their wealth or influence but in their knowledge of the queen’s bloodline. With this knowledge, they subverted legitimate authority for personal gain, resulting in the decay of the once illustrious Halifax family and a scandalous royal secret.

But it was not all doom and wretchedness. For it was in this final riddle that we found redemption and our salvation. We discovered the hidden power of a forgotten people - servants, butlers, and paupers - who stood bravely against the tides of time, using their wisdom and knowledge to hold the reins of power. Theirs was a silent struggle hidden behind closed doors - a story waiting to be uncovered.

*In the darkness of our fears, we found light in our anonymous heroes who knew our greatest power lies not in wealth, influence, or greed, but in a hidden legacy - a story waiting to be acknowledged.
