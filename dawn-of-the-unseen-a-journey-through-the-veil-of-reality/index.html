Dawn of the Unseen: A Journey Through the Veil of Reality


The cold, harsh light of the full moon illuminated every corner of the abandoned warehouse. Ivan, a shy and resourceful young man in his early twenties, gripped the edges of the wooden table tightly. His eyes darted from one nearby object to the next, trying to peer beyond their mundane appearances and unveil the secrets they held.

Tonight, as always, the warehouse served as a waypoint for otherworldly travelers, who had come to trade goods, share stories, and strike alliances. These mysterious visitors were known as the “Unseen.” They were intelligences from parallel planes of existence, and there was one entity Ivan had been waiting to meet for quite some time—a sorceress from the realm of Thought.

As he glanced towards the entrance of the warehouse, a sudden gust of wind blew out the flame of his only source of light—an antique oil lamp. The darkness enveloped him like a suffocating veil, intensifying his anticipation. A sense of dread washed over him, and he wondered whether he was truly prepared to encounter one who transcends the boundaries of his understanding.

Something stirred in the shadows. Ivan’s fear was quickly overshadowed by curiosity. He summoned the inner strength required to bridge the gap between conniving doubts and daring hopes.

Suddenly, a deep, ethereal voice resonated through the darkness. “Ivan, my dear boy. Your pursuit of knowledge shall be rewarded tonight.” The sorceress emerged from the inky void, her translucent figure emitting an otherworldly glow.

“Welcome, Oh Wise One,” Ivan stammered, his voice barely above a whisper.

The sorceress’ features were indiscernible beneath an emotional haze. Her body lacked any evident demarcations—no clear feet or limbs, yet her presence exuded an unrivaled elegance. “Forgive my abrupt introduction. Time, in my world, has little meaning.”

Ivan hesitated for a moment, but his curiosity overcame his trepidation. “Please, help me unveil the secrets of the universe hidden within the mundane. Grant me the power to perceive more than what one can witness on foot, through a telescope, or with the most advanced computers.”

With a solemn nod, the sorceress agreed.

The warehouse once more filled with light—this time, it was a brilliant violet hue. Ivan felt his mind slipping, as if he was being pulled through his own mental veil. He irresistibly recognized that this process could lead to a terrifying descent into madness.

Suddenly, his perception shifted. The warehouse transformed before him. The mundane objects, which had previously been lifeless, now had a spark of latent energy. Reality’s everyday veil had been torn, and the invisible patterns of the world manifested within his sight.

The sorceress revealed a glimmering gemstone for him to study. With a simple touch, he could see the memory of the cosmic conflict etched within: the shifting of tectonic plates, the eons-long planning of the future selves of the constellations, and an intricate history of the people who had once owned the stone.

Time seemed irrelevant in the sanctuary of the sorceress’ power. Hours of intense study flew by like seconds. The world he found himself in danced in perfect harmony with itself, a delicate, intricate ballet of unseen entities and mysterious forces.

Under the tutelage of the sorceress, Ivan knew that he had found the first step toward bridging the gap between the conscious and the unconscious. This unexplored world filled him with both fear and fascination. Most importantly, he now had the power to return to the world of humans, carrying a new understanding of the Unseen.

He thanked the sorceress for her teachings, harboring an unspoken hope to one day return and uncover more secrets among the Unseen. But as he made his way back to a world hidden in plain sight, his expanded consciousness began to perceive the seemingly mundane world anew.

And so, Ivan the once-shy young man returned, forever changed by his journey through the veil of reality. No one would ever look at him—nor any aspect of life—quite the same way again, for he now stood on three paths: the tangible world of men, the elusive realm of Thought, and the breathtaking world just beyond human perception.

And yet, even with this new sight, knowing the cost of his inexplicable powers, Ivan realized this was only the beginning of his journey. It was a journey he had to undertake not only for himself but for all who dared to walk the difficult path from the known world to the Unseen.

His.odyssey. would be fraught with obstacles and wrapped in a cloak of mystery. But ultimately, as the first student of the Unseen realm, his legacy would inspire generations to rise above the veil and see the world anew.
