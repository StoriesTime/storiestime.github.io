It was a chilly winter's night in the late 15th century when the sun dipped into the horizon, signaling the beginning of a blood-soaked battle. His elderly eyes gleamed with determination, burning like the sun in the frosty atmosphere. But none knew, among the bloodied soils below, was a legion of valiant samurai warriors like Akira, maintaining their unquestionables-rich traditions for adherence to perfection.
