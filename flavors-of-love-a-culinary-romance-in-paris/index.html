As she worked, Sophie's mind wandered to the upcoming Night of the Quince, a revered event that celebrated the art of French patisserie. The evening was a time for top chefs to showcase their creativity and culinary skills, and Sophie had been chosen to create a bespoke dessert to be served at the City Hall's grand ball. Sophie's thoughts turned to her childhood memories of helping her grandmother in the family bakery.
