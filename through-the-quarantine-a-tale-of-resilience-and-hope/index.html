We received the unexpected call around 6 pm, the first Thursday in March. "You’ll be required to self-isolate for at least 14 days. Your family will be provided with a comprehensive package containing food staples, toiletries, and medical supplies." The officer on the phone spoke with a tone that was firm but empathetic. As I listened, fear crept in. Never had I thought I'd be locked away in my own home. A limbo that welcomed no visitors and threatened self-annihilation. Self-imposed separation and lack of interaction with friends, family and the world. Chapter 2 - Quarantine Delusions The quarantine zone was widespread, affecting countless sections of the city. News outlets spread a consistent stream of excitement and anxiety about the rapidly changing times. Our house would quickly go into a fog encompassing the lockdown times. Suddenly, tweets and reports of the different celebrity responses and adoption of unforeseen coping strategies filled social media.
