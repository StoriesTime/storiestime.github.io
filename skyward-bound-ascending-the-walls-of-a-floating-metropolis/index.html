Skyward Bound: Ascending the Walls of a Floating Metropolis


In the year 2140, humanity had mastered the art of sky floating cities, an architectural marvel, soaring high above the clouds. One such city was Zephyr, a floating metropolis abundant with skyscrapers, bustling with life, and innovated to the core. Sky lifts zipped through the air offering quick transports from one towering building to another. Zephyr was the epitome of human engineering but deep within its hearts lay a hidden secret, a story that very few knew and even fewer dared to speak.

Lauren, a curious 10-year-old girl, lived in Zephyr soaring high above the clouds, her wide eyes gazed up to the sky lifts that crisscrossed the atmosphere. With her ever so curious mind, she always asked questions that intrigued everyone around her. One day while reading old manuscripts, she stumbled upon a hidden passage that was once erased from the popular history books. The words engraved in old ink sewed a coarse tale of the city’s beginnings, sinister secrets that the powers that be were desperate to conceal.

At the very birth of Zephyr, a group of scientists known as the Seven set out to build a floating metropolis. As construction progressed, it was discovered that the very foundation of their creation was shrouded with a strange and dangerous energy. They realized they had unearthed a dormant power that they couldn’t control. To avoid a catastrophic fall, the Seven devised a plan to seal the power away.

And they did. They hurled the wrath of their new discovery deep into the core of the city, entombing it in the heart of Zephyr. From then on, the Seven ruled the city without question or competition, nipping any talk of the power in the bud. Unknowingly, Lauren had stumbled upon the key that could potentially change her world forever.

As more pages unraveled before her enquiring eyes, she discovered that each member of the Seven belonged to a different lineage tracing its roots back to ancient civilizations. They each bore a genetic code that linked them to a long-forgotten power, a force thought to be nothing more than a myth. As the students of their knowledge, they were chosen to be the protectors of this energy, a burden passed down through generations.

Lauren could hardly believe her eyes, riveted to every word. And everyone assumed Zephyr operated under the rule of its esteemed contributors. Little did the world know, it was the city itself that truly held power.

Compelled by her thirst for the unknown, she decided to do what no one had dared to, discover the truth. Every night, she’d sneak out into the damp and dark alleyways, deep in the heart of the city. She followed a series of instructions hidden in a code, deciphered only by her keen intellect and determination. At last, she found it- an ancient artifact, pulsating with energy hidden in the overflow reservoir, gathering strength from the city’s core. This was the source, the key to unlocking the boundless energy that drove the city.

Printed in tattered pages, an old ritual presented itself to her. It detailed the ancient rites used by her ancestors to harness the power that flowed through their veins. Lauren’s hands trembled as she studied the symbols on the artifact, deciphering the language of her forgotten heritage. Enrolling in the highest gifted academy of the city, Lauren began to develop a secret plan to unlock and control the power without falling prey to its wrath.

Months turned to years, and with each passing day, Lauren grew stronger, her knowledge expanding beyond the traditional realms of the academics. She formed a bond with a few like-minded individuals, friends who shared her passion for unearthing the forbidden.

One afternoon, a chilling rumor reached her ears. The powers that be had discovered her secret, and an operation was underway to thwart her and her peers. Panic-striken, Lauren called for a top-secret meeting, revealing the truth she had uncovered. Together with her friends, they formed a pact to unlock the power and bring justice to the city.

The day came when Lauren finally dared to harness the dormant power of the metropolis. With a mixture of terror and exhilaration, she etched the inscription onto the ancient artifact. The groans of the city alive as if the streets themselves howled in defiance. A blinding flash of light erupted from the reservoir, enveloping the city in a cocoon of energy. Lauren and her friends collapsed, overcome, the hollow echo of the artifact’s climax reverberating around them.

As the energy ebbed, Lauren’s eyes fluttered open to the sight of her city, now susprended in an otherworldly gleam. It hovered with the iridescence of a cosmic dance, pulsating with the hidden power that it had been concealing for centuries. Slowly, Lauren stood up, the artifact in her hand.

Zephyr had found its true heart.
