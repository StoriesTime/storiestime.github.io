The Lost Lullaby: A Bedtime Adventure for Young Dreamers

3 min · 562 words

Lily was a child who loved music dearly. She could easily remember any melody or lyrics of a song. But this time, she had a special challenge. She dreamed of finding a song she had never heard before, a song that could only be heard under the starry night sky.

Chapter 2: The Journey Begins

莉莉决定了，她要开始寻找这个秘密的歌曲。她从自己的屋子里走出，穿过小镇，来到一个叫做“梦想之路”的小径。她已经听说过这个小径，可以让人找到他们的梦想。但是，这个小径非常陡峭，莉莉需要用很大的勇气来走下去。

Lily decided to start her journey. She walked out of her house, crossed the village, and arrived at a path called “Dream Path”. She had heard that this path could lead people to their dreams. However, the path was very steep, and Lily needed a lot of courage to climb it.

Chapter 3: The First Encounter

莉莉走了一会儿，遇到了一个奇怪的老人。他穿着一身黑色的衣服，脸上带着一个苍白的面具。他问莉莉，她是来寻找什么的。莉莉告诉他，她想找到一个秘密的歌曲。他告诉她，这个歌曲是非常难以找到的，但是他可以给她一个提示。

Lily walked for a while and met a strange old man. He was wearing a black robe and had a pale mask on his face. He asked Lily what she was looking for. Lily told him she was searching for a secret song. He told her that the song was very hard to find, but he could give her a hint.

Chapter 4: The Hint

老人告诉莉莉，她需要找到一个叫做“梦想之泉”的地方。他说，这里是歌曲的源头。莉莉问他，怎么才能找到这个地方。他告诉她，需要找到一个叫做“梦想之灯”的小灯，然后将其带到梦想之泉。

The old man told Lily that she needed to find a place called “Dream Spring”. He said that this was the source of the song. Lily asked him how she could find this place. He told her that she needed to find a small lamp called “Dream Light” and bring it to Dream Spring.

Chapter 5: The Second Encounter

莉莉继续她的旅程，来到一个叫做“梦想之灯”的小店。她走进店里，看到一个小灯放在桌子上。她问店主，这个小灯是不是梦想之灯。店主告诉她，这个小灯确实是梦想之灯，但是需要她付出一个条件。

Lily continued her journey and arrived at a small shop called “Dream Light”. She walked in and saw a small lamp on the table. She asked the shop owner if this was the Dream Light. The shop owner told her that this was indeed the Dream Light, but it required a condition to be paid.

Chapter 6: The Condition

莉莉问店主，条件是什么。店主告诉她，需要她在梦想之泉唱一首歌。莉莉问他，什么歌曲。店主告诉她，需要她唱出她最喜欢的歌曲。

Lily asked the shop owner what the condition was. The shop owner told her that she needed to sing a song at Dream Spring. Lily asked him what song. The shop owner told her that she needed to sing her favorite song.

Chapter 7: The Dream Spring

莉莉拿着梦想之灯，来到梦想之泉。她唱出她最喜欢的歌曲，歌曲的音符在空中浮现。她唱完歌曲，梦想之泉开始流出一个美丽的歌曲。莉莉听到了这个歌曲，她知道这是她梦想中的歌曲。

Lily took the Dream Light and arrived at Dream Spring. She sang out her favorite song, and the notes of the song appeared in the air. She finished singing, and Dream Spring began to flow with a beautiful song. Lily heard the song and knew that this was the song she had been dreaming of.

Chapter 8: The Lost Lullaby

莉莉唱完歌曲，梦想之泉继续流出歌曲。她唱了一会儿，歌曲的音符开始消失。她知道，这个歌曲将会消失在梦想之泉中。但是，她已经记住了这个歌曲的旋律和歌词。她回到了家，开始写下这个歌曲的歌词和旋律。

Lily finished singing, and Dream Spring continued to flow with the song. She sang for a while, and the notes of the song began to disappear. She knew that the song would disappear into Dream Spring. However, she had already memorized the melody and lyrics of the song. She returned home and began to write down the lyrics and melody of the song.

Chapter 9: The New Lullaby

莉莉写完歌曲，开始唱给她的家人和朋友们。她唱了一会儿，大家都被歌曲的美丽所吸引。从那天起，莉莉的家人和朋友们每天晚上都要听她唱这首歌曲。莉莉知道，这首歌曲将会被传递下去，并且将会成为一个新的安眠曲。

Lily finished writing the song and began to sing it to her family and friends. She sang for a while, and everyone was attracted to the beauty of the song. From that day on, Lily’s family and friends would listen to her sing this song every night. Lily knew that this song would be passed down and become a new lullaby.

Chapter 10: The Last Encounter

莉莉继续唱歌曲，她的声调变得越来越柔和。她唱完歌曲，遇到了那个奇怪的老人。他告诉她，这首歌曲将会被传递下去，并且将会成为一个新的安眠曲。莉莉感谢老人的提示和帮助，感谢他让她找到这个秘密的歌曲。

Lily continued to sing the song, and her voice became softer and softer. She finished singing, and met the strange old man again. He told her that this song would be passed down and become a new lullaby. Lily thanked the old man for his hint and help, and thanked him for letting her find the secret song.
