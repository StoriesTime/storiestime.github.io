Forbidden Romance: Strangers Destined to be Villain and Hero

Serena was a skilled healer, anointed by the gods to mend the wounds of her people. She possessed the unique ability to draw upon the life force of those close to her, a privilege that came with a dark price. Her skills bought her the respect of her fellow villagers and the begrudging admiration of a certain Watcher that had been tasked to keep watch on her.
