Grab Your Passport: Top International Festivals to Add to Your Bucket List


It all started one ordinary morning. Whitney, a young travel enthusiast, was browsing through her social media feed when she stumbled upon a picture of Europe’s most vibrant autumn festival, Oktoberfest in Germany. Captivated by the idea of dancing under the stars, sipping on frothy beer, and enjoying traditional Bavarian delicacies, Whitney decided to add Oktoberfest to her burgeoning bucket list. Something inside her stirred; a desire to experience the magic that only global celebrations could provide.

Chapter 2 - The Research

Whitney’s eyes were glued to her laptop screen as she researched the most coveted festivals around the world. Travel blogs, forums, and YouTube travelogues ultimately led her to a treasure trove of events waiting to be experienced: NYC’s energetic Halloween Parade, Argentina’s sizzling Tango Festival, the lively and colorful Holi celebration in India, Ibiza’s electronic music extravaganza that is Sunrise, the meditative Bhutanese festival, Punakha Drubchen, and, of course, the enchanting Rio de Janeiro Carnival.

As she jotted down dates and locations, she occasionally looked out the window, watching the world outside her little bubble blur by while her eyes rested on her expanding list.

Chapter 3 - The Itinerary

Step by step, Whitney began piecing her dream trip together. She booked long-stay flights, reserved rooms in cozy guesthouses, and mapped out transportation networks. Devising a wish list enabled her to mix and match her newfound passion for unraveling global cultures with her newfound friendships from around the world.

Chapter 4 - The Adrenaline

Whitney’s heart raced as she approached each new vista, her insatiable wanderlust propelling her forward into an unparalleled expedition of self-discovery at each corner of the globe. She found herself immersed in vibrant street art at Buenos Aires’ Street Art Festival, with a kaleidoscope of colors enhancing her ever-growing collection of cherished memories.

Chapter 5 - The Revelations

During her passage through Italy for Earthka, the initiative for a sustainable future, Whitney encountered a community of kindred spirits. Together, they indulged in organic food, lively conversations, and deepened worldviews, bonding over their shared goal of safeguarding the planet for future generations.

Chapter 6 - The Growth

Every year of her journey brought its fair share of challenges: tight budgets, trying languages, and the occasional clash of societal norms. Yet, at the heart of it all, Whitney remained steadfast in her dreams. For each obstacle, she found opportunity - be it mastering the ancient Spanish tango or capturing the dizzying magic of Ibiza’s Sunrise on camera throughout the night.

Chapter 7 - The Unity

As the years stacked up, Whitney unearthed a powerful truth: the grand universal language at these events had less to do with words and more to do with the spirit of humanity that united each attendee. The hysterical laughter during Comedy Fest Japan interacting with comedians and audiences, the collective sickly-sweet anticipation forModuleName: the scent of sugary treats at Basel’s Switzerland Fasnacht Carnival, and the force of resolve found during Buenos Aires’ AAPI Heritage Month - it was these connections that proved invaluable to her continued exploration.

Chapter 8 - The Encore

After visiting 27 countries and participating in 71 unique festivals, Whitney realized that her bucket list was infinite. The promise of new horizons and adventures drew her ever onward - reminding her that some journeys never end.

Bringing her wanderlust delicacies and diverse expressions of human life, she now pens her wanderlust chronicles as chapters for other globetrotters seeking inspiration.

The Wanderlust chronicler sits back, smiling, as she looks at the stack of stories growing by the day, and suddenly, another spark of curiosity ignites in the corner of her heart, beckoning her to embark on the road less traveled… again.
