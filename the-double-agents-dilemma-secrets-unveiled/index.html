Chapter 4: The Price of Truth

The following days were a blur. Adrian had gone into hiding, using every skill he had learned as an agent to erase his presence from the world. He destroyed all evidence of his past life, erased every trace of the missions he had completed. But it wasn’t enough. They were always watching. They knew he had made the decision to walk away.

The world he had once known had turned into a battlefield, with each side using his name as a weapon. And now, Adrian Mills was a ghost—forgotten by those who once trusted him, hunted by those who never would.

One fateful morning, Adrian received a message from Emma, the only person who could still reach him. It was simple, cryptic, and chilling.

They know.

Epilogue: The Final Reckoning

Adrian knew the price of truth. It wasn’t just the sacrifice of a career, a reputation, or even a life—it was the loss of everything he had once believed in. The agency he had worked for was not the savior of nations, but the architect of chaos. And in this world of espionage, where alliances were fickle and trust was a currency too expensive to spend, Adrian had learned the hardest lesson of all: no one is ever truly free.

The truth had cost him everything.

But in the end, it had also set him free.
