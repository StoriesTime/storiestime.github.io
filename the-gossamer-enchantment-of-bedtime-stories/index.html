The Gossamer enchantment of Bedtime Stories


In an age long forgotten, a quaint village nestled at the foot of an ancient forest would each night gather ‘round the village elder’s fireside. Though there were many firesides throughout the village, none favored the warmth and wisdom of the elder’s more than the others.

One evening, as the fire crackled and danced in the flickering light, the elder began to weave a tale of the village’s origins as they knew it. The tale began: “In the days that time has since washed away, the village was but a sapling adrift amongst the colossal trees of the forest, searching for its footing and reaching for its place in the world.”

The tale continued, as each person leaned in closer to hear the words of the storyteller, the village elder, Nana Idina who with every rhythmic gesture, spun the tale of the village’s beginnings into a grand and captivating narrative.

“The village elder, a wise and kind-hearted crone, had the gift of foresight and a knowing of the ancient songs of creation. As she shared the legends with the village, she observed a recurring song that foretold the village’s growth and prosperity. Though the people hungered for more tales, Idina insisted upon the silence of the song she heard, fearing the village might be consumed by the storms it would weather.‚Äù

Nightingales sang their lullabies throughout the dark sky, their melodies suspended in the air, hovering like a silken thread above the villagers’ heads. For generations, they whispered these songs in the cradles of newborns and the bedsides of elders as they drew their last breaths. These threads bound the village like silk ribbons to the ancient trees, and they lured the forest’s other creatures to nest among the village’s many diverse roots.

This was a quiet and loving village, forged from the threads of those enchanting bedtime stories that wove together the lives of its people. The tales were secrets passed down from one generation to another, a collective tapestry that held the roots of the village deep in the earth and the branches of the largest trees nearly touching the moon.

Families shared these bedtime stories throughout their lives, sewing the love and knowledge of their lineage into the hearts and minds of the villagers. As the years passed and the stories continued to grow, the village did too. With each tale, the bonds became stronger, and with each generation, the people understood more of their roots and place in this world.

Unbeknownst to most of the villagers, however, there was a power within those stories that the village elder had kept hidden from her kin. This power had weighed heavily upon her heart for many years, as she felt the burden of the whole village upon her shoulders. It was her absolute duty to guard the secret of the ancient song, never to share it.

One night, under the shroud of a moonlit sky, whispers of the mysterious power within the bedtime stories began to spread among those who were curious. A shadowy figure, Qerash, had discovered the enigmatic nature of the stories and sought to bend this perilous power to their will. He gathered a group of equally cunning and sinister individuals, all drawn to the allure of this shadowy power within the stories.

This group of rogue storytellers devised a plot to steal the ancient song from the village elder, thinking to harness its power for their own nefarious purposes. Their desire was to alter the village’s long-held bedtime stories and twist the enchantment to define their own sinister ends.

The village elder, sensing a darkness in the air, could no longer hide the truth from her people. As the whispers of Qerash and his corrupt followers grew, the power of the past’s grand tapestry wove the villagers together more tightly than ever.

Taking her siting in front of the fireside once more, her voice carried a cryptic urgency as she shared the truth: “My dearest villagers, there is a power within these bedtime stories that I have kept hidden from you all. I have shielded you from this song because it was a double-edged blade, one that I believed would be best left unsung. A darkness is coming, led by those who wish to bend and warp this ancient power, and it is up to us now to stand together against this threat.”

Heeding the elder’s words, the villagers prepared for the impending storm. United by their tales and their love for one another, they built a fortress of protection for their village, weaving and reweaving the threads of their collective history.

One by one, Qerash and his conniving followers tried to breach the village’s defenses. Though each attack was repelled, the darkness continued to grow, threatening to overwhelm the once tranquil village.

In the village elder’s final moments, she revealed the secret of the ancient song, allowing the villagers to harness its power in defense against the shadows that surrounded them. The binder of the ancient stories was removed, allowing the true power of the tapestry to be revealed.

As Qerash and his minions began the final assault, the villagers sacrificed their own lives to protect the village. Their love for one another and their devotion to the stories that bound them drove them to take hold of the threads in their last moments, calling forth the full power of the song.

The village burned that eve, but the threads of the enchanting bedtime stories remained, binding the village to Qerash’s dark memories, making it a place of whispers and shadows. To this day, strangers still hike to the outskirts of the forest, hearing the faint echoes of ghostly melodies. They carry within them the whispered tales of a love so powerful that it transcended even the most tragic of endings.
