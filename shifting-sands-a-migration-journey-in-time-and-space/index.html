Shifting Sands: A Migration Journey in Time and Space


In the heart of a vast Sahara desert, under soft golden sands that danced with each gust of wind, lay the secrets of a civilization long forgotten. This was the cradle for generations of ancient Saharans and their curious practices of time and space manipulation. As the sun scorched the Earth, Hammada, a curious young boy, wandered by the shifting sands. He observed the rhythmic undulation of the dunes and couldn’t help but wonder about the mysteries buried deep within them. Little did he know, destiny had plans for him.

Fascinated, Hammada began to press down on the sand with his palm, only for it to re-emerge just a few inches away. He tried again and again, each time the sand shifting further, expanding in various directions. Great were the gasps, and laughter erupted among the onlookers who had gathered. However, it was this very funny sight that captured the attention of El-Mehtar, a wise and enigmatic old man. Intrigued, El-Mehtar came towards the boy and, with deft fingers, traced the patterns Hammada left in the sand.

“Ah,” whispered El-Mehtar, “You have been touched by the ancient magic of our ancestors. Welcome, young one, to the realm of sand-shifting.” And so, began the unorthodox education of Hammada - a calling he could scarcely believe.

El-Mehtar explained the secret knowledge passed down the generations from the time-bending marabout. Once a year, during the fleeting moment of the Solstice, a narrow chasm in the time-space continuum would open. One could shift sand to ‘walk’ through the chasm and emerge in a different era or location, a practice known as ‘Caid Migration.'

Now more than ever, Hammada was drawn to the ancient wisdom, craving the chance to experience a new world, untouched by the passage of time. In that decision was the birth of an extraordinary journey, an unfathomable quest into the heart of time, through the very shifting sands he once toyed with.

Exhaustive days and nights were spent honing his sand-shifting prowess, traversing the silken sands, leaving footsteps that crisscrossed like veins in a leaf. Hammada’s visions jaunted to eras teeming with fearsome creatures and epic battles, touch-and-go encounters with phantoms of ages long past, and worlds untouched by the strife of Earth. He dreamed of the day when he wielded the full force of El-Mehtar’s teachings and stood tall among the great marabouts of shifting sands.

The night before Hammada’s destined flight, he laid on the dunes and, for the first time in his life, marveled at the hypnotic glitter of stars in the sky. The ethereal beauty stirred a sense of longing in Hammada - a desire to walk among their ancient fellowship, to witness their forces at play as guardian spirits of time and space.

As the Solstice morning arrived, hammada scuffled a thin, elongated path ending in a peculiar crescent carved into the sands - his gate to time and space. With each phase, he felt the air around him grow restless and his heart thunder in symphony with the dunes. At last, Hammada uttered the ancient words El-Mehtar had taught him, “Fir’awn, la zar, la zamán,” meaning “King, pass through time, pass through space,” as he brought his palm down upon the sands.

As time seemed to freeze, Hammada felt his body lift off the ground, his essence traveling, ripping his existence apart, assembling and reforming in a brand new era. He had become one with the sands, effortlessly traversing across time’s intricate lattice, discovering ancient relics and civilizations long-lost to the sands of the shifting desert.

As Hammada reveled in his wondrous discovery of the mysteries the sands concealed from those who dared to seek them, he understood that he was now a steward of what lies within, and ultimately embraced a life he could have never otherwise imagined.

The stories of Hammada’s time-space mollusc travels were whispered into young ears in the arid nights of the Sahara. As the sands whispered tales of lost worlds and civilizations, Hammada became an emblem of freedom and adventure, beckoning the generations to come, emboldening them to journey along the shifting sands of their dreams.
