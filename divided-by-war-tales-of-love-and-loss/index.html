Divided by War: Tales of Love and Loss


In the once-peaceful lands of Eridoria, war had come, shattering the lives of its people and leaving behind a legacy of love, loss, and longing. The narrative of a nation torn apart by conflict is not unfamiliar, yet the specific stories of love and loss that emerged in the aftermath of war are lesser known. It is these tales, echoing through time, that whisper the human cost of war and the struggle to rebuild a shattered identity. The Divided Hearts of Lyra and Kael In the midst of war, amidst the chaos of conflicting ideologies, there lived two people, Lyra and Kael. Their story began innocently, amidst the splendor of Eridoria's pre-war era, where love was a language everyone spoke and understood. Lyra, a skilled healer, and Kael, a gifted warrior, from different backgrounds yet brought together by fate. Their love grew amidst the chaos of developing hostilities, fostering a sense of safety and love in a world falling apart. Lyra's heart, with its tranquil forest colour, symbolized peace, while Kael's, with its fiery burgundy hue, represented passion. Their love became a beacon of hope, shining brightly in the heart of war. But war divided them. In their attempt to seek peace, they found themselves caught in the midst of their families being on opposite sides of the conflict. Their love, became another layer of the antagonism that existed between the two groups.
