Whispers of the Forest: A Modern Retelling of Little Red Riding Hood


In the heart of the Whispering Woods, a dense emerald forest teeming with ancient secrets, the village of Oakwood was a sacred haven. Carved into the banks of a crystal clear river, the village's rickety wooden houses blended seamlessly into the forest, as if Nature herself had laid the foundation stone by stone. Here, amongst the carefully tended gardens and harvesting farms, there lived a young woman named Aria. Aria's distinguishing feature was her striking scarlet hair, a trait inherited from her great-aunt Celine, a brave witch known for her enchanted gifts. People in Oakwood believed that Aria's hair held hidden charms, capable of whetting the woodcutter's blade and rehydrating parched earth.
