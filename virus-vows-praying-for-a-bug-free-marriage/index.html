Virus Vows: Praying for a Bug-Free Marriage


Once upon a time, nestled in the heart of an enchanting city, resided the extraordinary inhabitants of Luckyville, each indelibly unique in their mannerisms and beliefs. Amongst this peculiar fellowship lived Ada, a twirling, 28-year-old ballerina, who commenced every morning by dancing to Johann Sebastian Bach, and Marty, a reserved, fortunetelling, 30-year-old hermit, engaged in guiding citizens through the inexplicable enigmas of existence.

Their paths would have never crossed if it weren’t for the grand annual festival of Unisonia - a three-day event cheered in by throngs of people, celebrating community spirit through a myriad of eccentric rituals and games. One such match, the Virus Vows Game, caught Ada’s eye, oddly entwined with her deepest desires for love and romance.

On an unusually warm autumn day, Ada arrived at the Unisonia grounds to sign up for the Virus Vows Game, controlled by the remote-controlled, spinning wheel of chance - a colossal essence symbolizing fate itself. She twirled around in her embroidered ballet slippers and envisioned her life with the phantom of an ideal partner.

Having observed Ada’s determination to emerge victorious under diverse circumstances, Marty stepped forward: “I shall share my foresight with thee and help thee procure love.” Intrigued, Ada secretly hired Marty to provide guidance as she pursued her dreams of love through participating in Virus Vows.

The alembicated spell Marty chanted began to weave lingering air vibrations and a luminescent path of cosmic energy unfolded itself at Ada’s feet, illustrating her life. Marty began to narrate her distant past where she had cowered away from romantic encounters and her present, where Ada craved a soulmate’s touch. “Fear not, dear Ada. For, eternal love awaits thee,” declared Marty.

Unisonia had reached its climactic finale, where bubbling laughter and melodious cacophony filled the halls. Ada and Marty, together, joined these cacophonous delight when it came time for the Virus Vows spin.

With eyes closed, palms clutched towards the heavens, she took a deep breath and spun the wheel. The turbulence gave away to a sudden ceasefire as the wheel came to a rest, defeating chaos. A triumphant grin adorned Ada’s face as she discovered her prize: Fate had granted her the opportunity to dance through life with her true love - Marty himself.

Under the rejuvenating sunlight of the following morning, Ada awoke to an assortment of flowers, stuffed animals, and love letters - gifts of adoration from Marty, who donned dancing attire and performed a delightful ballet routine. Admiration welled up within her heart - the revelation that love was found in the most peculiar, unexpected forms.

That same evening, beneath the star-cloaked sky, Ada and Marty intently gazed at one another with unwavering hearts. They exchanged digital contracts, coalescing their souls in an unhinged, unyielding virus vow, echoing throughout the realm of Luckyville.

In the days that followed, Ada and Marty delved headlong into the dense tapestry of committed partnership, traversing through deep conversations, shared dreams, and a bevy of enchanting moments only found in the most extraordinary kind of love. They emerged as the setting sun, refining their love together.

Through this artistically sur-mesured Virus Vow, the beginning of an extraordinary era, crafted by Ada and her mystifying mate Marty, unfurled its allegiance. Mutual understanding, a dance of love, and breakthroughs in their personal growth bloomed through boundless serendipity.

And so it was told, through word of mouth, in the unmistakably bewitching, off-beat city of Luckyville - the legend of Virus Vows, the savior of their marriage - a testament to the everlasting pursuit of love and the birth of a magical symbiosis between two extraordinary souls eternally enthralled in the dance of devotion.
