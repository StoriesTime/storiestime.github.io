Amidst the din of morning commute, Emily Wilson stifled a yawn as she trudged towards the office. Never one for impromptu scribbles on napkins, Emily compassed her focus tightly, unobscured. Little did she know, her life was about to resonate with an unpredicted note. As she entered the elevator, a stranger brushed past her, sharing a spontaneous glance before dissonantly laughing at some private joke. Emily married the laughter with fleeting curiosity, relegating thoughts of this fleeting interaction to the oblivion of her packed schedule, an overmind disconnected from such events. Barely a few minutes into her day, a sudden jolt pushed both of their worlds into close harmony. A passing hoverboard, lied astray, stumbled into her path.
