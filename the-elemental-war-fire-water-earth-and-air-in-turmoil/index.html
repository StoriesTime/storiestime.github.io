The Elemental War: Fire, Water, Earth, and Air in Turmoil


In the realm of Eridoria, where the fabric of the universe was woven from the threads of four elemental forces, a catastrophic war had begun. Fire, Water, Earth, and Air, once in balance, had been sparked into chaos by the manipulations of a group of rogue mages. These sorcerers, fueled by ambition and a thirst for power, had discovered a dark art that allowed them to seep into the hearts of the elements, corrupting their once-harmonious bond.
