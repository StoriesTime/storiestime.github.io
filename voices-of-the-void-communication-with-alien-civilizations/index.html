The room vibrated with anticipation, as if the universe itself held its collective breath in reverence. "Aurora's advanced quantum entanglement receiver has picked up a tenfold increase in energy fluctuations," Edward announced, his voice charged with injection of curiosity. "These readings suggest an extraterrestrial signature."
