Interdimensional Intruder: A Reality Rewind


In the quiet suburban town of Serentia, nestled in the cradle of the Appalachian foothills, everything seemed routine and mundane. For the most part, the people of Serentia were bound by tradition and routine, valuing consistency and stability above change. However, the summer of 1995 brought with it a truly unexpected and unpredictable guest, one that would shatter the very foundations of Serentia’s unshakeable certainty.

Upon the commencement of what promised to be a glorious summer, Daren Hollister embarked on his usual morning bike ride through the sprawling emerald fields fringing Serentia’s outskirts. The sun, anew from winter hibernation, shone with an unrelenting brilliance that painted the town an untouched golden hue. However, it wasn’t this idyllic panorama that caught Daren’s attention, but rather, the peculiar hissing that emanated from a nearby thicket, an unfamiliar sound, almost pulsating in rhythm, interrupting the pastoral silence.

Cautiously approaching the source of the hiss, Daren gasped in shock at what he found: a bizarre extraterrestrial creature completely unlike anything he had ever seen before. This creature, no larger than a domestic cat, appeared to be entwined in a symbiotic relationship with a thin transparent umbilical cord, its source a shimmering portal, around 4-feet across, that materialized seemingly out of thin air. Entranced by this ethereal dance, Daren spent the rest of the morning watching the interdimensional intruder – as he would come to call it – interact with its temporary habitat.

Word of this extraordinary discovery spread rapidly throughout Serentia, in much the same way as the interdimensional portal had emerged. In the days that followed, hordes of residents thronged to the outskirts to catch a glimpse of the celestial visitor. School attendance soared, neighborly gatherings burgeoned, and even Serentia’s most unlikely characters suddenly found themselves at the eye of a peculiar vortex of praise.

As the unusual creature endeared itself to the people of Serentia, local deputies established a buffer zone around the portal to preserve its fragile ecosystem and promptly contacted the United States military’s top-secret research facility, Horizon Base, seeking guidance on how to maintain public order and safely procure the interdimensional anomaly. Arriving with stealth and utmost secrecy, the base’s rapid responders set up a temporary headquarters in the heart of Serentia to conduct expedited research and establish rules for visitor interaction, much to the surprise, confusion, and occasional chagrin of the townspeople.

The interdimensional intruder, known collectively as Zephyr, seemed unfazed by the influx of new faces and quickly adapted to its adopted home. In a quirk of circumstances, the once overlooked creature became Serentia’s most beloved native, celebrated in colorful murals and immortalized in local legends. Likewise, news of Zephyr’s extraterrestrial existence garnered global attention, polarizing the world’s populace into two camps of thought: those who sought to exploit Zephyr’s presence for profit and power, and those who sought to preserve the vulnerable being for the mysteries it held.

As public sentiment surrounding Zephyr and its cosmic origins clouded, an enigmatic figure emerged: Dr. Vincent Cross, the leading authority on interdimensional phenomena and Zephyr’s most ardent defender. Affectionately known as the Protector of Serentia, Cross dedicated himself to unearthing Zephyr’s history and understanding the nature of its arrival. By studying the creature’s behavior, Cross devised a series of protocols aimed at safeguarding it from those who sought harm, and the people of Serentia looked upon him with a sense of gratitude and trust.

However, the dawn of a new epoch brought an intrinsically sinister force to Serentia in the form of Amalgam Energy Incorporated (AEI), a massive conglomerate enterprising enough to harness the unknown and extract its potential for profit. Lacking any moral compass, AEI sent one of their most ruthless agents, Cyndi Magnex, equipped with every tool available to apprehend and compel Zephyr into their clutches.

Dr. Cross, ever vigilant and steadfast in his duty, was wary of the looming threat that AEI posed to not only Zephyr but to the community’s way of life. When Magnex’s forceful tactics blackened Serentia’s skies with the lingering stench of ulterior motives, Cross drew upon the unified front of the townspeople and devised one desperate final gambit.

To protect Zephyr and Serentia from AEI’s inevitable aggression, Cross – supported by the townsfolk – devised the Reality Rewind, a bold, innovative technique targeting the interdimensional portal itself, in direct tandem with Zephyr’s fragile existence. Explaining that the Reality Rewind would compress time within the portal and Zephyr’s domain, enabling them to escape the ever-present peril posed by AEI, Cross led his allies to seamlessly implement the plan.

Upon execution, a mesmerizing whirlwind of cosmic energy crashed over the town, funneling the reality of the portal into a tight vicious cycle. Zephyr, its existence inextricably intertwined with the portal’s cosmic mechanisms, was spirited away to an unfathomable expanse, free and unblemished. As the whirlwind began to dissipate, the people of Serentia realized just how intimately their lives had been entwined with Zephyr’s celestial voyage.

With the once unthinkable, now eagerly welcomed, Serentia seemed consumed with the joy and solace that accompanied the reality rewind. The inhabitants embraced a renewed sense of community solidarity and a deeper understanding of the secrets and mysteries that lay just beyond their town’s now seemingly mundane borders. Amidst the pervasive jubilation, a sense of hope pervaded, as the people of Serentia cautiously looked toward the horizon, dimly aware that they were, in fact, only at the beginning of a truly cosmic chapter in their collective history.
