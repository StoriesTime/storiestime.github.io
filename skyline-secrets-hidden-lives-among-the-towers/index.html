Skyline Secrets: Hidden Lives Among the Towers

Home to the city's elite, this gleaming steel giant seemed impervious to the tribulations of the common folk. Yet, nothing could be further from the truth. The city's renown administrators had trivialized the concept of social hierarchy, convincing the average citizen that equality was within reach. Little did they know, life was anything but equal among the city's towering skyscrapers.
