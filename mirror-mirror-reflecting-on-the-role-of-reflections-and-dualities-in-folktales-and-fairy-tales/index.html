Mirror, Mirror: Reflecting on the Role of Reflections and Dualities in Folktales and Fairy Tales


Once upon a time, in a land far, far away, there was a village of weary souls seeking solace and answers. Among them stood a peculiar old woman with a mirror as big as her eyes, who would often showcase her wondrous artifact to the villagers. This enchanted mirror reflected not only the appearance of the person gazing into it, but also their deepest thoughts, desires, and volition.

One fateful day, a young maiden ventured into the village seeking a solution to her father’s persistent sickness. Intrigued by the old woman’s mirror, she approached and asked, “Mirror, mirror on the wall, what consequences shall I face if I refuse my father’s heart’s desire?”

The mirror gently swirled with wisps of light, revealing layered depths of the maiden’s psyche. In the core of her reflection, she beheld a hidden chamber adorned with shadows and riddles. Encrusted within this cryptic chamber was the answer she had sought. An ancient duality: to embrace a fate bound by love, or to seek refuge within a world of cold detachment.

Taking into account the consequences of each path, she chose to dive into the depths of her reflection to confront the riddles hidden in her heart. With determination burning brighter than her father’s sickness, she swam through her inner world, guided by her own instincts, wisdom, and love.

Throughout the village, word of her quest spread like wildfire, sparking the curiosity of a mischievous young lad who thirsted for adventure. Chasing tales of hidden treasures, he came to learn of the maiden’s passage into her reflection. He could not decide whether to follow her path or forge his own. But something within him felt an undeniable connection, as if drawn by unseen threads into the depths of her journey.

Lured by destiny, he too embarked on his own adventure, albeit veering off the prominent path, seeking hidden answers down untrodden trails. As he ventured deeper into himself, face to face with his own fears and desires, he found himself struggling to navigate through the labyrinth of his inner universe.

Their paths inevitably crossed, the maiden and the young lad simultaneously emerged from their own reflections, dappled in wisdom and resilience. The enchanting mirror revealed their innermost selves to each other, unfolding the intricate tapestry of their shared fate.

Conversely, a cunning sorceress grew envious of the mirror, viewing it as a dangerous tool for the villagers to recognize their own desires and potential. She devised a wicked plan to control the mirror and weave a web of deception around it.

The sorceress infiltrated the village, transforming herself into the old woman’s mirror’s peaceful visage. Under her daunting influence, the mirror malfunctioned and unveiled a distorted truth, stealing the village’s strength, hope, and unity. It fed upon their emotions, twisting their realities and plunging them into darkness and despair.

Brave and resolute, the maiden and the young lad forged an alliance, taking it upon themselves to repair the tattered fabric of the village’s spirit. Each determined to search out the root of this darkness, they turned inward once more, delving into their reflections to mend the innate duality that threatened to tear them apart.

After countless trials, they eventually discovered a hidden door in the heart of the enchanted mirror, leading to an immeasurable realm where the sorceress held sway. It was here that they confronted her wicked machinations and fought to restore the power of the mirror and retrieve the lost essence of the village.

The maiden and the young lad’s unbreakable bond transcended their individual fears, desires, and shadows. Working hand in hand, they dismantled the sorceress’s deceptions and brought light back into the hearts of the village. In the end, the two emerged from their sacred journey stronger, bolder, and more alike than ever before.

The enchanted mirror, now cleansed of the sorceress’s taint, returned to being a reflection of one’s true self, a vessel of wisdom, and a reminder of the power of facing the darkness within. United by their experience, the young maiden and the young lad embraced, as the village rejoiced in newfound strength and harmony.

Through their dedication, they discovered that reflection is not just the simply the mirror of one’s soul, but also a gateway to overcoming dualities and embracing the unity found in the heart of one’s true self. For the people of that village, reflecting on the role of reflection became a cherished tool in embracing both light and darkness while striving for a life guided by love, wisdom, and unity.
