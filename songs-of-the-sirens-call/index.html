Songs of the Siren's Call


Once, in a distant kingdom, there was a legendary group of musicians known as the Songs of the Siren’s Call. They were believed to be the first and only musical ensemble to possess the magic of song that could charm the heart. Their enchanting music could soothe, calm, and heal all emotional wounds, captivating even the coldest hearts.

As their music enchanted the crowds, the king grew envious of their popularity. With a jealous heart, he summoned the group to the palace and commanded them to play for his amusement, under the pretense of silence and an initiation. The band agreed, little knowing the perilous consequences.

No sooner had the musicians begun to play than chaos ensued. Their transcendent music was too alluring for the king’s liking. The palace guards were bewitched by the tune and became delirious, setting off an uncontrollable and violent commotion in the palace.

With pandemonium reigning, the band hastily fled from the palace, leaving their instruments behind. As they ran through the streets, the people of the kingdom succumbed to the tunes of the Siren’s Call, letting out piercing shrieks and cries of joy. The king was incensed and ordered his guards to hunt down the musicians before they could ever play again.

The group scattered, never to be heard in harmony again. But the legend of the Songs of the Siren’s Call lingered on. In the deepest forests and most hidden valleys, fragments of their enchanting tunes remained. These echoes of their former glory continued to enchant those brave or foolish enough to venture into the wilderness and seek the remnants of their magical music.

The king, realizing the band had not been captured, paced the palace grounds bewildered. He longed to silence the haunting melodies that tormented his sleep. Desperate for a solution, he summoned the kingdom’s darkest and most cunning sorcerer, the Seer of Sorrows.

The Seer, intrigued by this challenge, embarked on a perilous expedition to capture the echoes of the Siren’s Call. Armed with mystical instruments, he sought to gather the fragile shards of the band’s enchanting lyrics and chords. Night after night, he ventured into the darkness, tracking each melodic fragment with cold calculation.

After weeks of painstaking research, the Seer at last discovered the secret to the Songs of the Siren’s Call’s magic. The music that enthralled the kingdom was no ordinary tune, but one composed of the purest, most precious, and almighty element never before harnessed - Emotionalia.

Determined to seize this newfound power, the Seer fashioned a magical instrument known as the Lamentatore, designed to capture the elusive Emotionalia. With the Lamentatore in hand, he ventured into the darkness once more, intent on subduing the Siren’s Call forever.

But the Seer underestimated the strength of the Siren’s Call’s magic. In the ensuing battle between the Seer and the enchanted melody, the Lamentatore shattered into countless fragments, scattering the Emotionalia across the kingdom.

Realizing the futility of his endeavors, the Seer relinquished his quest. In his place, an unexpected outcome occurred: the kingdom, cured of its infatuation with the Songs of the Siren’s Call, found peace in the harmony and simplicity of everyday life.

The remnants of their magical music continued to echo through the ages, serving as a reminder to the power of the Songs of the Siren’s Call, and the consequences that unfettered passion can bring.
