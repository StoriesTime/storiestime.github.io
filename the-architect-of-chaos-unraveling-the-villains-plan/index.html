However, beneath his polished demeanor, a cauldron of resentment brewed, simmering against the societal constraints that stifled his true potential. Feeling confined by conventional norms, he began to question the very foundations of the world. Thus, the seeds of chaos were sown. Chapter 1: The Architect's Design Within the confines of his secured lair, Vex poured over his schematics, nourishing the seeds of an insurrectionary ideology. Vex's vision was nothing short of revolutionary: maximization of power through efficiency of destruction. Cities were to be reimagined as intricately designed organisms, their structures folded, and rebuilt to withstand turbulent conditions. Buildings would be engineered to yield to calculated devastation.
