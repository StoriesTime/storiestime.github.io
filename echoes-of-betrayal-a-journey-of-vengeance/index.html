Echoes of Betrayal: A Journey of Vengeance


I remember the oath I took with my brother: to venture together into the unknown, to avenge the loss of our loved ones and friends. But as time went by, our oath was gradually forgotten. Until one day, I received a letter that read: “Is your oath with your brother still valid?” I opened the letter, and what I saw was my brother’s photo and a simple phrase: “We haven’t completed our task yet.”

我感到前所未有的愤怒和仇恨。我知道，我和我的兄弟的誓言还在，我们的任务还没有完成。我决定，开始了我的长途之旅，寻找我的兄弟和我们的敌人。

I felt an unprecedented anger and hatred. I knew that my oath with my brother was still valid, and our task was still unfinished. I decided to embark on a long and arduous journey to find my brother and our enemies.

第二章 遗忘的记忆(Forgotten Memories)

我记得我和我的兄弟曾经的生活，我们的家，我们的朋友，我们的梦想。但是，这些记忆渐渐地被遗忘了。直到有一天，我来到了一所老旧的房子，我看到的是我的家我的家人的照片。我感到前所未有的感动和愤怒。我知道，这是我的家，我和我的兄弟曾经生活过这里。

I remember our life, our home, our friends, and our dreams with my brother. But these memories gradually faded away. Until one day, I came to an old house, and what I saw was my family’s photos. I felt an unprecedented sense of nostalgia and anger. I knew this was my home, where my brother and I once lived.

我开始了我的搜索，我寻找我的兄弟和我们的敌人。我走遍了整个城市，遇见了许多人，但是没有一个人知道我的兄弟的下落。我感到前所未有的沮丧和绝望。

I started my search, looking for my brother and our enemies. I walked through the entire city, meeting many people, but no one knew my brother’s whereabouts. I felt an unprecedented sense of despair and hopelessness.

第三章 遗忘的誓言(Forgotten Oath)

我记得我和我的兄弟曾经的誓言，但是现在，这誓言渐渐地被遗忘了。我感到前所未有的沮丧和绝望。我不知道我该如何继续下去。我不知道我该如何找到我的兄弟和我们的敌人。

I remember our oath with my brother, but now it’s gradually being forgotten. I felt an unprecedented sense of despair and hopelessness. I didn’t know how to continue. I didn’t know how to find my brother and our enemies.

但是，我突然想起了一件事。我记得我和我的兄弟曾经说过的一句话，我们说过，我们的誓言永远不会被遗忘。我感到前所未有的希望和决心。我决定，继续我的搜索，我将找到我的兄弟和我们的敌人，我将完成我们的任务。

But suddenly, I remembered something. I recalled the words my brother and I once said: our oath would never be forgotten. I felt an unprecedented sense of hope and determination. I decided to continue my search, I would find my brother and our enemies, I would complete our task.

第四章 遗忘的记忆(Forgotten Memories)

我继续我的搜索，我寻找我的兄弟和我们的敌人。我走遍了整个城市，遇见了许多人，但是没有一个人知道我的兄弟的下落。我感到前所未有的沮丧和绝望。但是我没有放弃，我继续我的搜索，我将找到我的兄弟和我们的敌人。

I continued my search, looking for my brother and our enemies. I walked through the entire city, meeting many people, but no one knew my brother’s whereabouts. I felt an unprecedented sense of despair and hopelessness. But I didn’t give up, I continued my search, I would find my brother and our enemies.

终于，我找到了一条线索。我知道，这是我的兄弟留下的线索。我感到前所未有的希望和决心。我决定，继续我的搜索，我将找到我的兄弟和我们的敌人，我将完成我们的任务。

Finally, I found a clue. I knew this was my brother’s clue. I felt an unprecedented sense of hope and determination. I decided to continue my search, I would find my brother and our enemies, I would complete our task.

第五章 遗忘的誓言(Forgotten Oath)

我找到我的兄弟，我们一起走向死亡的彼岸，我们的誓言终于被完成。我感到前所未有的感动和愤怒。我知道，我们的任务终于完成，但是我也知道，我们的誓言还没有完全完成。我感到前所未有的希望和决心。我决定，继续我们的任务，我们将完成我们的誓言。

I found my brother, and together we ventured towards the unknown, our oath was finally fulfilled. I felt an unprecedented sense of nostalgia and anger. I knew our task was finally complete, but I also knew our oath was not yet fully fulfilled. I felt an unprecedented sense of hope and determination. I decided to continue our task, we would complete our oath.

我和我的兄弟一起走向死亡的彼岸，我们的誓言终于被完成。但是，我知道，这还不是结束。我知道，我们的任务还没有完全完成。我感到前所未有的希望和决心。我决定，继续我们的任务，我们将完成我们的誓言。

I and my brother together ventured towards the unknown, our oath was finally fulfilled. But I knew this was not the end. I knew our task was not yet fully complete. I felt an unprecedented sense of hope and determination. I decided to continue our task, we would complete our oath.
