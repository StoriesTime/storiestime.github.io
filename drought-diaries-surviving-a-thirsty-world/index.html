Drought Diaries: Surviving a Thirsty World


The scorching sun beat mercilessly on the parched earth as Cam close the rugged pickup truck door. The dust he stirred clouded the air, suffocating the little life that teetered on the brink of existence. He inhaled deeply, the aridness of the air stinging his nostrils as it starved for any hint of moisture.

Desperation had brought him to this desolate town in the southwestern part of the country that was once an oasis of life and abundance. “Surviving a Thirsty World,” as most jaded optimists called it nowadays. The sign at the entrance of Greystone, now half-hidden by the cacti and graffiti, read: “Welcome to Greystone: Where Every Drop Counts.”

Cam wiped his brow, agitation evident in the sweep of his arm. He shouldn’t have been surprised; the rest of the world was just as dire. But still, a speck of hope beat in his chest, the faintest flicker that perhaps here, in this forsaken land, a solution lurked, a hope for the survival of humankind.

Chapter Two: The Diaries

With a weary step, he entered The Water Bazaar, an air-conditioned refuge. Inside, vendors haggled loudly, displaying their meager supplies of water and advanced-tech gadgets designed for water conservation. The conversations around him crackled with slight desperation, despair chasing hope, an eternal game in this thirsty world.

He walked like a man bewitched, his eyes scanning the shelves. Suddenly, he spotted them – the Drought Diaries, bound in worn brown leather. A sense of familiarity struck him. These were supposed to be legendary; each of them contained the secrets of survival, handed down from generation to generation.

The price ticket on the Drought Diaries listed an exorbitant amount. But Cam had one last chip in his pocket, the only remnant of his former life in San Francisco, his last bargaining chip. Keeping his voice steady, he asked for the owner. Atop a ladder, the man regarded him with sharp eyes before climbing down.

Chapter Three: A Deal with the Keeper of Diaries

“Name’s Jeb,” the man introduced himself, his handshake firm. “You another traveler seeking the fabled diaries?”

Cam nodded. “That I am. I have something that might interest you,” Cam began, extracting his last safeguard - a state-of-the-art water filter system, still wrapped in plastic.

Jeb’s interest was piqued, a hint of a smile curling his lips. “Apocalyptic haggling. I’ve missed this,” he chuckled. “Your turn, pal.”

After a tense negotiation, the deal was sealed and the Drought Diaries were Cam’s. A sense of relief washed over him as he clutched them.

Chapter Four: Unraveling the Secrets

Back at his motel, Cam leaned against the wall, the coolness an invitation to explore the treasures he had borne for years. The dry leather creaked as he opened the first page. The knowledge he sought oozed from each page, offering him the hope he anxiously craved.

He learned about ancient water-conservation techniques, the wisdom of the ancients that lingered within the pages. The knowledge revitalized his spirit, breathing life into his faltering hope. Cam prayed that it would be enough, that their existence could be salvaged from this arid morass.

His resolve hardening like the parched earth awaiting the first rain, Cam gathered his belongings and bid Greystone farewell. He had a date with destiny: to breathe life into the barren and revive the dying world.

Chapter Five: Water, the Essence of Life

Months passed, turning into years, as Cam traversed the searing lands with the Drought Diaries beside him, every lesson a stepping stone in a persistent quest to reclaim the planet’s breath.

His teachings took root in parched lips and the wayward greens sprouted hints of life. The whispers of the Drought Diaries spoke of cooperation over competition, fostering communal bonds that prevailed amid hardships. Through these teachings, Cam nurtured the spirit of unity that lashed against the winds of desolation.

Epilogue: The Dawn of Renewal

The world no longer seemed so thirsty when he thought back on the lessons imparted by the diaries. The parched earth drank in his teachings, the expansive skies weeping their mercy at last.

Years later, standing upon the newly flourishing meadows, Cam breathed deeply. The air was moist with life, no longer a parched weeful of the world’s past.

The Drought Diaries, once thought to be volumes of esoteric lore, now found their place in the world’s history. A testament to man’s resiliency and his unwavering determination to reclaim the lost essence — Water, the lifeblood of the earth.

About Author:

The story of Cam and the Drought Diaries reflects a unique future where man, once relentless in his pursuit of destruction, begins to heed nature’s plea. Intrigued by a world ripe with possibilities, and armed with the spirit of sustainability, Cam impacted lives across the breadth of the globe through the ancient Roman and Native American conservation techniques as well as the designs of successful Swiss scientists.
