My supervisor, Karen, calls out to me from her desk, "Morning, Joe!" I grin and wave, already anticipating the many interactions I'll have with my colleagues throughout the day. My coworkers affectionately refer to me as "The Office Jester," and I'm proud to earn my keep. 8:30 AM - Morning Mugging I begin my rounds, visiting each department to spread some cheer.
