The Last Sorceress: Unraveling the Cursed Legacy


In the realm of Tenria, where the skies were perpetually painted with hues of crimson and gold, and the land itself seemed to whisper secrets to the wind, the great sorceress, Lyraea, once wielded unparalleled power. Her mastery over the arcane arts had saved the realm from the brink of destruction, earning her the reverence of her people and the envy of her rivals. However, the very threads of her greatness would become the source of her downfall. Lyraea's illustrious career spanned centuries, each year delving deeper into the mysteries of magic, and with each new discovery, her essence grew more intricate, her presence a symphony of whispers and silences. Yet, amidst her triumphs, an event would occur, forever altering the course of Tenria's history. During an attempt to imbue a mortal with her own magical prowess, Lyraea's thoughts volunteered themselves to the task, and a curative idea supplanted her foundations.
