The air was thick with the scent of damp earth and decaying leaves, and the eerie calls of nocturnal creatures pierced the darkness. They spoke little, knowing that the wilderness demanded respect and vigilance. Their expedition relied on worn trail maps, tracing the past footfalls of travelers who had ventured before them.
