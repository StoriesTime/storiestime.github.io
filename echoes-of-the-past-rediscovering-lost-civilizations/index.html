Deep within the heart of the Amazon rainforest, hidden from the world for centuries, lay the ancient city of Zenith. Once a thriving metropolis, home to a prosperous and advanced civilization, its existence had been relegated to myth and legend. They had, in fact, fueled her life's work, driving her to scour the dense jungle in search of the lost city. Sophia's journey began in 2010, when she stumbled upon an obscure manuscript penned by a 19th-century explorer. Although initially dismissed as fiction by colleagues and experts, Sophia's curiosity refused to wane. Together, they forged a theory that Zenith, the elusive city, was more than just a dream.
