With Nethros defeated, the world began to heal. The land, once dark and twisted, began to return to its former glory. The trees regrew, the rivers flowed once more, and the skies cleared. The guardians had saved the world, not through brute force, but through the strength of their unity and the light within their hearts.

As the sun rose over the horizon, the guardians stood together, their spirits stronger than ever before.

“We have done it,” Aerun said, her voice filled with pride.
