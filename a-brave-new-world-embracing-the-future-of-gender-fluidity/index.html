A Brave New World: Embracing the Future of Gender Fluidity


In a distant future on a faraway planet known as Novus Terra, the citizens of this utopian society were free from the shackles of rigid gender roles- they were all born gender fluid, a concept that was once foreign and intimidating to the inhabitants of Earth centuries ago.

Novus Terra was governed by the Council of Elders, a group of the wisest and most knowledgeable individuals who sought to maintain peace, unity and harmony in the community. The realm was divided into neighborhoods, each of which served a distinct purpose, imparting knowledge about various aspects of life, be it literature, science, or physical fitness.

As the sun-rays danced upon the sapphire blue skies, Lumina awoke in her family home situated in the heart of the Literature district. At the tender age of seventeen, the world was yet to unfold before her, full of endless possibilities.

Today marked another important day in their society, the beginning of Lumina’s exploration process, the rite of passage that would unveil her true purpose in life. As she stepped outside her home onto the cobblestone streets of her neighborhood, she was immediately greeted with an array of friendly faces and well wishes.

The journey to the Exploration Center was both nerve-wracking and exhilarating for Lumina. Upon reaching, she was greeted by her guide, Icarus.

“Lumina, welcome. Today, we embark on a journey to uncover the essence of your being,” said Icarus, smiling warmly.

The day was divided into sessions, each designed to delve deeper into the psyche and find the core beliefs and thoughts that defined Lumina’s identity. The first half focused on self-discovery, comprised of a myriad of experiences and activities that unlocked dormant traits, welcomed new perspectives, and encouraged a state of openness.

“‘Who am I?’ has been a question asked by generations before us, and resolving it will lead to a personal liberation that we have been striving to achieve,” Icarus said just as the second half of the session began.

It started with donning various costumes to immerse themselves in stances, postures, and gestures of different gender expressions. As Lumina transitioned from one form to another, she could feel her consciousness expand, giving her insight into how she felt most authentic in each one.

The climax involved an experiment using a Neuro-Predictive Device, specially designed to analyze an individual’s charging impulses and corresponding gender fluidity. As the device administered a Cortical Stimulus, Lumina was draped in a luminescent aura that began to glow brighter, projecting a multicolored spectrum that transitioned from blue to green to red. Icarus and the team were astounded at the sight and soon witnessed an emotional meltdown resulting from a sudden surge in revelation.

Once Lumina regained composure, a holographic projection emerged displaying her spectrum’s unequal distribution of colors. The concentration of each color correlated the strength of influence, meaning Lumina would express a mixture of masculine, feminine, and non-binary traits. These traits would define her purpose on Novus Terra.

“‘Lumina, your expedition indicates an extraordinary amalgam of traits. You possess the capability to bring about empathy and understanding to those in need of guidance. Your unique predisposition shall bring about great balance to the society we live in,” Icarus proclaimed with awe.

As the day came to a close, Lumina emerged a more mature and inspired individual. She understood that her fluid gender identity was not a curse, but a gift - one that could veer the course of Novus Terra to even greater heights of progress and enlightenment. The world was her oyster, and the color spectrum etched in her consciousness would forever guide her decisions, relationships, and her unending pursuit of success.

As the Council of Elders implemented changes in the societal paradigm, it became clear that gender fluidity was the true engine of this utopian world. Novus Terra moved forward embracing the future, one in which not only love and kindness would prevail, but so shall the freedom of self-expression without borders or limitations.
