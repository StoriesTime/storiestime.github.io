His eyes, a deep shade of indigo, seemed to hold a world of pain and loss within them. He was a man driven by a singular purpose: to find a new home for his people before it was too late. "This is it, folks," he announced to the crew over the comms system. "Our next jump destination is the Andromeda system, a speculated hub of life in the dying universe. But as the crew began to disembark and explore their new surroundings, they quickly realized that this was no ordinary galaxy.
