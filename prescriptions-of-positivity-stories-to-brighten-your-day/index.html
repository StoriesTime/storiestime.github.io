Prescriptions of Positivity: Stories to Brighten Your Day


Once upon a time, in a world not so different from ours, there existed a quaint little village called Positivity. This village was unique and vibrant, for it was home to the happiest and most optimistic people one could ever hope to meet. In Positivity, it was said that every house had a prescription of positivity, ensuring that all inhabitants radiated with zest and joy.

The village was governed by a wise and benevolent council, each member of which had a special power linked to their title. The village’s preeminent leader, the Mayor of Marvelous, had the extraordinary gift of turning even the gloomiest of moods into bright and sunny ones. The Chancellor of Cheer was gifted with the ability to spread laughter through their infectious and knee-slapping sense of humor. The Rear Admiral of Resilience had the awe-inspiring strength and agility to lift the spirits and abilities of others, making them feel strong and capable.

In the heart of Positivity stood the grand Positivity Palace, where the village’s wellbeing was monitored and maintained. The palace was adorned with windows that sparkled like sunshine and doors that always welcomed the village inhabitants. Inside the palace, a great team of Positivity Practitioners worked diligently day and night to ensure that every village inhabitant received their daily dosage of good vibes and positivity.

One day, a curious newcomer named Mimsy arrived in Positivity. Mimsy was not like the other villagers; they often found themselves lost in thought, bemused by the perpetual optimism of the village’s inhabitants. Mimsy, whose name was short for “Mysterious Mimsy,” was a scholar and an explorer with a burning desire to understand the world’s true nature.

Curiosity led Mimsy to the heart of Positivity Palace, where they encountered a bewildering sight: rows and rows of mysterious, pulsating bottles glowing within a well-secured vault. Intrigued, Mimsy decided to find the reason behind this astonishing phenomena, and so echoed the classic phrase, “Curiosity killed the cat, but satisfaction brought us cats.”

Mimsy managed to sneak their way into the heart of the palace, where they first discovered that the faintly glowing bottles contained the lifeblood of Positivity. A unique and rare elixir, this lifeblood symbolized the innate positivity of Positivity’s inhabitants. Mimsy’s intrigue piqued when they saw the manner in which the Practitioners administered the lifeblood. Far from the usual method, the Practitioners’ elegant touch ritual involved singing, dancing, and laughter.

After observing the procedure for several days, Mimsy realized that the lifeblood was not just coincidentally glowing; the bottles grew brighter, the more their contents were shared. This drew Mimsy into a fascinating mystery: What happened when the lifeblood was drunk?

Boldly, Mimsy decided to confront one of the Positivity Practitioners—the warm and Sociable So-and-So—seeking answers in their own words. Much to Mimsy’s surprise, So-and-So revealed that taking the lifeblood transformed the taker, empowering them with a feeling that was more than mere satisfaction. It filled the drinker with a wellspring of cheer that maintained their positivity and composure.

Intrigued by this unexpected revelation, Mimsy soon found themselves questioned by the village’s very own peacekeepers, the Peaceful Prowlers. Caught between a rock and a hard place, the Peaceful Prowlers first detained Mimsy for the violation of palace security, but soon released them with a newfound respect for the questing spirit that dwelled within.

Having gone over So-and-So’s head to establish this newfound discovery, Mimsy quickly realized the repercussions of their actions. The revelation now placed the village’s lifeblood prescriptions under a cloud of suspicion. So-and-So’s untenable position prevented them from continuing their usual duties, leaving the village’s inhabitants in a state of unease and curiosity.

And thus, Mimsy found themselves committed to a quest to understand the origin of Positivity lifeblood, all the while rectifying the troubles they had inadvertently created.

For Mimsy, the journey promised newfound wisdom alongside a profound fulfillment—a potent, intoxicating cocktail of curiosity and positivity. A life of adventure awaited, and all because the curiosity of one individual had enabled them to unveil the heart of an entire village. What lay at the end of this path remained veiled in mystery, but for now, the warm glow of uncertainty had firmly gripped Mimsy by their heart.

As a new day dawned, Mimsy began their trek across the village in search of information, following the trail of surprise revelations that the Positivity Palace had to offer.

Positivity isn’t always transparent, but its spirit is eternal

The village of Positivity rested on the cusp of uncertainty, standing on the precipice of a new understanding. With each passing day, Mimsy, the adventurer, sought to illuminate the mysteries that lay hidden within the heart of the village; the unraveling of a simple, crystal-clear elixir.

To this day, Positivity maintains its recipes and methods, as the village finds a way to embrace the unknown. Suspense and uncertainty can be a jolt to a society that has relied on stability, but the legacy of Positivity has not faltered. It is in these moments of suspense that the village learns and grows stronger. The village’s inhabitants are a testament to their resilience and adaptability.

The Positivity lifeblood might be shrouded in some mystery, but the spirit of positivity runs deep within. So, much like Mimsy’s journey that transcended the bounds of expectation, the force of positivity drives us forward. It brings us together in times of need and fuels our collective hope. Positivity isn’t always transparent, but its spirit is eternal.
