Chasing Dragons: A Journey Through the Karoo's Hidden Wildlife Haven


Karoo Township, a dusty sunset sprawled across the horizon, as shafts of golden light pierced through the wispy clouds. The sun’s embrace was soon to be replaced by the cool whisper of night’s breath. In this desolate corner of South Africa, nestled between the imposing Swartberg and reliance ranges, life thrived in the most unexpected places. Welcome to the Karoo, the beautiful, the strange, the hidden wildlife haven.

It was on the cusp of Autumn, 1984, when 14-year-old Tommy Mofokeng first heard the stories. The tales of dragons, ancient creatures believed to slumber in the earth’s depths, with eyes that glittered like amber and scales as hard as stone. When the time was right, they would awaken, roaring with fury and breathing flames that could melt metal.

Tommy, a curious young boy with sun-kissed skin and eyes that sparkled like the night sky, found himself consumed by the legends. His imagination soared, envisioning these magnificent beasts with unharnessed power. He would beg his father, a humbly-paid game ranger, to take him on a quest to discover the truth about these mythical serpents. But his father, a no-nonsense man of the earth, warned Tommy of the dangers lurking in these lands, the sweltering summers and biting winters, the loneliness and the unforgiving terrain.

But despite his father’s warnings, Tommy was determined to uncover the secrets of the Karoo. And that is where the real adventure began. He found himself a band of fellow adventurers: Hylton, a boy with a thirst for knowledge, Michael, a fearless explorer with a heart of gold, and Lerato, the quiet and observant one who could see what others could not.

Their journey led them beyond Karoo’s outskirts to the heart of the expanse, where the yellow grass stretched out as far as the eye could see. The first days were spent exploring abandoned homesteads and winding waterways, uncovering the history of the Karoo as the seasons turned.

Autumn moved into Winter and the cold winds sculpted the land, carving out secrets only known to those who dared to seek them. The air grew sharp with a cold that bit deep, yet it couldn’t diminish the yearning that burned within them. Tommy and his gang continued onward, each day more determined to uncover the dragons’ secret lairs.

At last, in the dead of night, during the coldest of winters, the stars’ light shimmered upon the hard-caked earth and the relentless cold subsided, the four friends came across a secret passage, half-hidden by a twisted shrub. The entrance, dark and foreboding, lay at the end of a desolate ravine that narrowed like a throat, closing behind them as they ventured within.

The passage, barely wider than a single file of human footsteps, wound deeper into the earth. The walls pressed in with suffocating force. Tommy and the others had to press their backs against the stone to make their way forward.

Hours passed, or perhaps it was days – time ceased to exist in this underground kingdom, devoid of light, the air thick with age-old secrets. The passage finally opened onto a chamber, its walls adorned with crystalline veins that sparkled in the darkness.

Gathering their courage, the adventurous foursome ventured further. As their eyes adjusted to the darkness, the nature of their discovery became disturbingly clear. Deep within the earth’s bowels, they found the dragons – ancient and spectral – their skeletal forms unmistakable. The dragons were not fire-breathing beasts of myth, but a species of prehistoric dragons, their horned heads lowered, their once-mighty wings folded, as if they carried the weight of the world on their shoulders.

Beneath the skeletons, scattered amidst the bones and dust, lay the shards of pottery, iron, and remnants of metal. This was the dragons’ lair, the place where they had slumbered, undisturbed for millennia. Though their time was long past, their presence echoed throughout the chambers that guarded their secrets.

Tommy and his friends spent weeks exploring the hidden world beneath the earth, mapping it all out, and collecting samples of the ancient dragon’s remains. And with the passage of time, they regaled their tales of adventure and discovery, igniting the imaginations of countless others who came to seek out the dragons of Karoo’s hidden wildlife haven.

As the sun dipped below the horizon of the Karoo once more, that dusty, sun-kissed land that bore witness to so many secrets, the stories of dragons never faded away. Though the bones of these ancient creatures lay hollow and silent in their chambers, they lived on in the spirit of Tommy and his friends, adventurers who dared to chase the impossible, and in doing so, uncovered the true magic of the land.
