With a team of skilled conservators, she embarked on a perilous journey to restore the ancient temple. The group's efforts, however, were met with indifference by the local authorities, who viewed the temple as just another item on a long list of urgent priorities. "But what about the significance of this site?" Khadija asked, exasperated, during a heated meeting with the city officials. "It's merely a relic of a bygone era, Dr. Ahmad," they retorted. "There are buildings in disrepair that require more immediate attention." Khadija's eyes widened in dismay. "You're reducing an ancient source of wisdom to nothing more than a pile of rubble. If you continue to neglect sites like this, future generations will be left with nothing."
