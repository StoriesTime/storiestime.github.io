The Puppet Masters: Behind the Political Curtain


In the world of politics, nothing is as it seems. Beneath the surface of a seemingly functioning democracy, there exists a sinister network of puppet masters with their hands grasping the strings of power. Their influence is subtle yet omnipresent, shaping the course of history to suit their own agendas. The Birth of a Puppeteer Dr. Elizabeth Parker was a renowned psychologist with a penchant for understanding the intricacies of human behavior. Her work mostly revolved around criminology, but a peculiar incident during her doctoral research shifted her focus. She discovered a peculiar phenomenon - individuals who could articulate the intentions behind seemingly innocuous actions, as if they held some sort of insight into the collective psyche of a community. Intrigued, she devoted her life to uncovering the source of this ability. Her quest led her to the obscure world of hypnagogia and the pioneering work of Professor James Thompson. Thompson had been an early advocate for the therapeutic use of hypnosis, recognizing its potential to liberate unwanted thoughts from the subconscious. Elizabeth's research convergence with Thompson's ideas initiated a revolution in her understanding of the human mind's capacity for control. Weaving the Web of Deception Years of unearthing through research eventually lead to the discovery of a top-secret organization operating behind the political aftermath. The Group, as Elizabeth dubbed them, was barely perceived through the veer of transparency its figurehead embodied. Whoever they were, how they formed, or why they pursued such control kept her intrigued. She appointed an ace undercover operative, Adam.
