Leaving Home: Migrant Stories of Struggle and Triumph

The Journey: Struggles Along the Way

Many migrants endure dangerous conditions, sometimes placing their lives at risk, all fueled by a hope that the destination will offer a life free from the hardships of their origin.

Arrival: The Challenge of Adaptation

The Continuing Cycle
