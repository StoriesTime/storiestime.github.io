Sonia graduated from Yale second in her class, highlighted by countless awards, honors, and distinctions. In 1992, she was appointed as a judge to the U.S. District Court for the Southern District of New York – a remarkable accomplishment for a Puerto Rican girl from Bronx. She adeptly managed this newfound position while dealing with the challenges of juggling a fulfilling personal and professional life.

Despite ongoing challenges, Sotomayor forged ahead.
