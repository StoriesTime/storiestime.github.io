As I stood in front of the attic, its old wooden door creaked when I pushed it open. The air was thick with dust, and I could barely see through the thick layer of cobwebs hanging from the ceiling. Memories flooded back as I stepped inside, memories of the countless hours my grandmother spent here, tending to her belongings, and secrets. In the corner of the room, a beautifully crafted wooden chest caught my attention. Its intricately carved lid was made of a rich dark wood that seemed to reflect the shadows of the room.
