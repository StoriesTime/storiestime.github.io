Rift Rewind: Voyages to Variant Eras


Hidden deep in the wilderness of Oregon’s Siskiyou Mountains, nestled among ancient redwood trees, lay the unassuming entrance to the time portal known as Rift Rewind. It was discovered by chance in the early 21st century, and ever since the world has been captivated by the unlimited possibilities that lie within its swirling vortex.

Escalating curiosity soon led to the establishment of Rift Rewind: Voyages to Variant Eras – an organization funded by the richest, the most daring, and the most scholarly individuals in the world who wanted nothing more than to explore the myriad paths that history and alternative timelines had carved out.

As you might imagine, adventurers who agreed to work for Rift Rewind were a diverse bunch – archaeologists, historians, biologists, and even the odd magician, who you might say had a knack for understanding the many threads that intertwined to form the fabric of reality.

Our story begins one particularly quiet evening in the year 2024 when our protagonist, Alex Moreau, a historian by trade, was going through a hard time. His research into the life and times of American outlaw, Billy the Kid, had hit a series of dead ends, and he was days away from losing his funding. Then he stumbled upon an advertisement for Rift Rewind: Voyages to Variant Eras. The capability to travel through time and meet the real Billy the Kid in person seemed too good to be true, but desperate times called for desperate measures.

With no time to waste, Alex made his way through dense forests, bypassing ancient ruins and long-forgotten civilizations, until he arrived at the Rift Rewind headquarters. Surrounded by rugged mountains and enveloped in a shroud of verdant mystery, the compound was reminiscent of the world’s most exclusive summer camp. Recruits were made to live communally and share all possessions in an effort to foster unity and the camaraderie necessary for their unique profession.

Qualifications mattered little when it came to joining Rift Rewind; instead, the organization vetted candidates based on their mental, emotional, and physical fortitude, confident that those who had the strength to navigate the challenges of time travel would ultimately prove the most successful.

Determined and highly skilled, Alex passed every test with flying colors. After a series of extensive preparations, including practical training in multiple historical eras and advanced data-gathering techniques, Alex was ready for his mission.

In the early hours of a fog-drenched dawn, Alex took a deep breath and gazed into the swirling vortex of the Rift Rewind portal. With a swift, decisive leap, he plunged headlong into the churning, light-filled maelstrom.

The tingling sensation of transportation was overwhelming, and Alex felt his body discombobulate – a brief but seemingly endless moment of disorientation griping his senses. Then, he was through, and the world around him morphed into what apeared to be a bustling late-19th-century Wild West establishment.

Having arrived in the era of the legendary Wild west outlaw, Alex wasted no time in seeking out Billy the Kid. He soon discovered that the Kid and his gang were hiding out in a small valley north of town, using an abandoned ranch as their hideout.

And so, Alex set out to rendezvous with the infamous criminal, preparing to immerse himself in a history that lay shrouded in mystery, hoping to glean truths from the outlaw himself.

The long-awaited meeting occurred one starlit evening in the dusty, windswept valley. The Kid was not what Alex had imagined: young, charismatic, and intelligent beyond his years. Over a rudimentary meal, they swapped stories, laughing and bonding as they discussed everything from the challenges of outlaw life to the intricacies of Civil War history.

Nevertheless, it was the experiences shared in the shadows of that remote ranch that ultimately informed Alex’s academic career, opening new pathways in his research and enabling him to further questions into the very nature of time and history.

But for now, these intimate moments of history-crafting were bountiful – Alex was living his passion, and each day brought new discoveries that would forever change the course of his destiny.

To this day, the exact nature of the Rift Rewind portal remains a mystery to the outside world, and the early pioneers who made history-changing expeditions remain enigmatic figures, hidden in the shadows of their extraordinary achievements.

These pioneers navigated the impossible, exploring the untold stories of the past, present, and future, crafting the seeds for humanity’s future understanding of time and our place in it.
