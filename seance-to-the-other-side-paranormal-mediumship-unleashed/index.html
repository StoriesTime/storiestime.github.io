Seance to the Other Side: Paranormal Mediumship Unleashed


Olivia held the old, musty smelling Ouija board in her trembling hands as she sat alone in her dimly lit room. Her eyes couldn’t escape the glaring red numbers on her clock: 3:33 AM – the sign she always ignored, but tonight was different. She needed answers.

Her little sister, Emily, had passed away six months ago in a tragic accident that left Olivia heartbroken and confused. Because of the sudden death, a darkness enveloped Olivia’s life. She could no longer find solace in the realm of the living and was desperate to communicate with Emily in any way possible. It was then that she stumbled upon the dusty Ouija board hidden deep in the attic.

Captivated by the prospect of connecting with Emily again, Olivia set up the board on her coffee table and guided her fingers over the planchette. With her eyes closed, she took a deep breath. “Emily, I need you,” she whispered. “Please guide us to the other side.”

The board rattled, fear coursed through Olivia’s veins, and her nails dug into the polished wood. The planchette began to move. Shivers ran down her back as she watched in awe and foreboding.

“Emily? Is that you?” Olivia pleaded. Slowly, her eyes followed the path of the planchette as it formed letters.

I AM NOT EMILY, it spelled out. Olivia felt her heart race as uncertainty flooded her mind. She could hear the faint whisper in the room, speaking only to her. Intrigued, she centered herself and continued.

“What do you want?” Olivia wrote, her fingers shaking on the planchette.

I WANT… OWN HER. The words hung in the air as darkness seemed to cloud the room. Olivia’s breath caught in her throat.

Receive her hospitality and promises of guidance, or realize the harrowing truth of the paranormal world?

Olivia hesitated. Little did she know that the spirit world would soon pull the curtain back on its dark, mysterious secrets…

The events of the past six months replayed in Olivia’s head. Memories of her childhood with Emily. The joy, the laughter, and the unconditional sisterly love. It all felt like a cruel ruse that her mind insisted on torturing her with at this most crucial moment.

She had always been the brave one – that’s why Emily looked up to her. That’s why she persuaded Emily to accompany her on adventures to the lush forests or desolate sunsets. And now, without her, the world felt more daunting than ever.

But despite the pain of loss and fear of the unknown, the spirit, unlike Emily, offered some semblance of connection. It offered an unnerving reminder of the other side. And that reminder was more tempting than Olivia could bear.

With hesitation seeping out of her, Olivia decided to summon the spirit again.

“What do you want from me?” she asked, the planchette trembling beneath her fingertip.

LISTEN…

Time felt unyielding as Olivia waited for the spirit to reveal its sinister plot. She could only focus on the tick-tock of the clock.

…TO THE TRUTH. Olivia watched in dismay as her fingers spelled out the words. The accented whisper seemed to tickle her ear, making every hair rise on end.

“Fear not,” Olivia heard, the voice unlike any she knew. “For to know the truth will set you free.”

Olivia gulped as she looked around her room, now shrouded in a darkness almost palpable. But the whisper grew louder, as if taunting her.

Command the darkness to avoid the demon’s embrace, Olivia scrawled out to the spirit. She needed to know that there would be no evil presence on the other side. Her heart raced as she awaited its response.

THE DARKNESS LISTEN TO COMMAND WANTS ONLY TO UNLEASH, it scribbled out. Now more fearful than she had ever been, Olivia felt herself clinging onto her sanity as she contemplated her next move.

“Why have you come here to me then?” she asked, hoping it would be the last time she communicated with this unsettling presence.

COME… COME TO THEM TOO. The wooden orb jerked and soon spelled out the final words.

THEY HAVE FELT YOUR REACH… Olivia felt sick as the realization hit her: her sister Emily, the life that she had lost, had also felt the spirit’s connection with her.

As her hands fell back to her lap, Olivia heard a faint whisper of Emily’s laughter within her mind. “You never gave up on finding me, Livvy. Thank you.”

Olivia’s heart fluttered to hear her sister’s voice so intimately within her thoughts. A second later, exhaustion washed over her, as if the encounter had drained the last of her energy.

The room grew silent, and Olivia marveled at the supernatural bond the spirit revealed she shared with her late sister. She knew she would not be returning to that shadowy realm anytime soon, but the promise of Emily’s grasp provided her a renewed sense of comfort and peace.

Exiting out of her room, Olivia returned the Ouija board to the attic, sealing it in a deep, black box. “I won’t need you again,” she vowed to the forgotten relic.

She never expected that darkness to resurface in more ways than one.

Living in her home alone, with no one present to challenge the truth she uncovered, Olivia feared for her life. She had summoned something sinister into her life, and she knew she could not trust that things would remain calm.

Desperate to find someone to validate her experiences, Olivia sought the help of a local spiritualist named Matilda.

Matilda, an old woman with milky eyes and frail hands, had known her fair share of supernatural encounters. Her wisdom and intuition led Olivia to believe she might recognize the spirit’s presence.

Peering into the flames of the fire, Matilda asked Olivia about her experiences, clearly intrigued by the knowledge of the spirit world.

After recounting the harrowing tale of her unexpected visitor, the whispers of darkness, and the strange connection to her late sister, Olivia braced herself for Matilda’s disbelieving stare.

“I see,” Matilda finally uttered. “Then you know this entity is no ordinary spirit.”

Her words nearly sent Olivia spiraling back into her fears, but Matilda’s wisdom never left her. “Have you ever done research on dark spirits?” Olivia asked, her voice quivering.

“A bit,” Matilda responded. “But it’s not something I take lightly. It sounds like you have a powerful force on your hands. I suggest learning everything you can about them.”

Thanking Matilda for her honest words, Olivia left her home, her head reeling with the knowledge and fear of what a dark spirit might be capable of. She dove into her research, determined to prevent future harm to herself and the spirits that inhabited her home.

Olivia soon realized that dark spirits thrived on chaos, disintegration, and manipulation. These malevolent entities craved connection, seeking hosts in vulnerable people or environments. True to Matilda’s words, she had to uncover the force behind her late sister’s connection and break it off—both for her own sake and for Emily’s eternal peace.

In countless late nights spent researching and seeking help from amateur spiritualists and seers, Olivia learned that dark spirits varied in their intentions. Some sought revenge or left a departed soul responsible for unfinished business. Emily’s passing, however, was tragic, with no signs of vindictiveness or unresolved emotions.

Still thirsty for answers, Olivia clenched her fists. The stress of grappling with reality had caused her friends and family to distance themselves. Paranoia now lurked at every corner as Olivia’s search for the truth intensified.

Her days became busy wandering the less familiar parts of town, her usual hangouts replaced by tight-knit spiritual groups practicing occult readings, meditation, and seances. But the search for answers to her sister’s connection and the sinister force proved to be elusive.

Some argued that dark spirits branched through ancestral curses, misguided souls left awry by vengeful ancestors with malevolent bonds. Yet it seemed implausible given Emily’s sudden disappearance and the sheer randomness of her death.

Others suggested that these spirits held grudges against the living, wishing to influence or control those left behind. But such thoughts seemed unlikely; Emily’s death was just an accident, without any unknown malice tied to her younger sister.

Desperation began to claw closer and nearer to the surface as Olivia wandered through the darkness of the spiritual world. She knew she could not escape her fate of confronting this dark spirit, but she could harness the power to understand its vindictive force.

After weeks of research and spiritual connections, Olivia finally crossed paths with a man who seemed genuinely knowledgeable and compassionate about the dark spirit world.

Marcus, a former skeptic turned expert in the paranormal field, spent years delving deep into the unknown, capturing spirits and binding them with rituals to protect the living and save unfortunate souls.

Marcus sat across from Olivia, his piercing green eyes reflecting the determination she suppressed in her own. Boasting a twenty-year tenure in his field, he had faced some of the most dangerous and wise spirits.

Olivia explained her family tragedy and her encounters with the dark spirit that seemed intent on ensnaring Emily and her life. Marcus’s deep understanding gave her the courage she sought, and she hungrily soaked in every piece of knowledge he offered.

After regaling Olivia with countless ghost stories, tales of vengeful ancestors, and other paranormal encounters, Marcus finally uncovered the chilling truth about Olivia’s situation.

“The dark spirit that sent you those cryptic messages is primarily seeking to cause chaos within your life,” Marcus explained. “Perhaps it recognizes the vulnerability you have experienced since your sister’s death. In this situation, their mission may not be an attempt to harm you, but to watch your life crumble, in hopes of capturing and merging with a new host.”

Olivia gasped, realizing how deeply this malignant spirit had intruded on her life. She’d been living in a nightmare she’d only beginning to uncover.

“These spirits may also be reaching out through the Ouija board, making use of the powerful tool for communication and influence,” Marcus continued, “This entity’s main goal may be to take control and possess one of your living acquaintances, seeking someone new to harass by channeling their darkness.”

The air seemed to grow colder as Olivia recalled the Ouija board and how it led her on a dark path to the unknown. Marcus pressed on.

“To make the connection go away, you must first break any link the Ouija board has made with spirits. Then, draw a line in the sand. Perform spiritual cleansing in your home and protect yourself with psychic shields. Finally, visualize a barrier that keeps the spirits at bay.”

Overwhelmed by his advice, Olivia felt both fear and determination mix within her. Marcus reminded her that many spirits were bound by love and protection from those they cared about, and with the help of the spiritualist community, they could help Emily rest in peace and save Olivia from the clutches of this dark spirit.

With Marcus’s guidance, Olivia began working on her psychic defenses, performing cleansing rituals both within her home and within her heart. She destroyed the Ouija board, eager to sever connections with malevolent spirits.

Olivia joined spiritual groups regularly, offering her story and partaking in mass-protection rituals designed to ward off evil. Even as she began to make progress, resolve hardened deep within her.

As the days passed, the whispers in the darkness receded. Olivia’s nightmares, once filled with strange spectral laughter and twisted shadows, began to dissipate. Empowered by Marcus’s words and her determination to protect herself and Emily, she finally felt a sense of progress in her fight against the unknown.

Time set its slow pace, healing the deep wounds left behind by her sister’s death and banishing the malicious spirits that previously haunted her. Olivia’s trust in the spiritual world strengthened, and she found new friends and allies in the spiritual community.

Emily’s death still lay heavy on Olivia’s heart, but the nightmares, the darkness – the unfinished bond – had ceased to be. And as she grew stronger within, Olivia vowed that she would never allow herself to succumb to the sorrow and terrifying realities that consumed her in those days.

And as she closed her eyes, one last memory of Emily, the worlds of the living and the dead, flickered into her mind: Emily, giggling and chasing her through the forest, offering her warm embrace, and whispering, “I love you more.”

As Olivia opened her eyes to the reality of her life, she knew that no matter how deeply the darkness burrowed beneath her skin, the love of her family would inevitably protect her from the abyss without a sound.
