The clock tower stood tall and proud in the heart of the city, its mechanical heartbeat echoing through the winding streets. For generations, the clock tower had been the epitome of timekeeping excellence, its intricate mechanisms a marvel of human ingenuity. But beneath its façade of beauty and precision, a secret was brewing - a plan to steal the very essence of time itself. Enter Our Hero, Timekeeper Maxwell "Max" Wellington was no ordinary clockmaker. With his deft hands and sharp mind, he had spent his entire life studying the intricacies of timekeeping. His fame as a master clockmaker spread far and wide, drawing the attention of a mysterious organization known only as "The Syndicate." They had a proposal for Max, one that promised to change the course of history forever. Max stood in front of his small workshop, a miniature version of the grand clock tower that stood mere blocks away. The door creaked as he pushed it open, revealing a narrow stairway that led to the clockmaker's lair. He gazed up at the collected hordes of cuckoo clocks and intricately carved timepieces lining the shelves, each one whispering secrets of the past. With a flick of his wrist, he paused time, causing the hour hand to hang motionless in mid-air. He flashed a mischievous grin as he sidestepped the clock's proteins, flitting from gear to gear with an ease born from countless hours of study and tinkering. As he reached the transfer stage, the moment when the past converges into the present, the locks could sense his impatience, testing him, daring him to equalize with an appearance which choked the silhouette.
