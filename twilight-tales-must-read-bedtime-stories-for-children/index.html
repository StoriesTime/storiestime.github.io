Twilight Tales: Must-Read Bedtime Stories for Children

3 min · 623 words

As the warm summer night fell, a little girl named Little Red Riding Hood prepared to visit her grandmother’s house. Her grandmother lived on the edge of the forest, far from Little Red Riding Hood’s home. Her mother had given her some food and a letter, telling her to be careful on the way.

小红帽离开家门，步行穿过森林。森林的树木很高，叶子发出柔和的 rustling 声音，仿佛在欢迎她。小红帽感受着森林的温暖和宁静，感到非常高兴。她继续前进，直到来到一所小屋前。

Little Red Riding Hood left her home, walking through the forest. The trees were very tall, and the leaves rustled softly, as if welcoming her. Little Red Riding Hood felt the warmth and tranquility of the forest, and she was very happy. She continued forward, until she came to a small cottage.

这所小屋 belongs to一只名叫狼的生物。他是一个很善于伪装的生物，曾经骗过很多人。他看到小红帽，感到很高兴，想到可以骗过她。他对小红帽说：“你好，Little Red Riding Hood，我是你的外婆，我住在这里。”

The small cottage belonged to a creature named the Wolf. He was a very skilled deceiver, who had fooled many people before. When he saw Little Red Riding Hood, he felt very happy, thinking that he could fool her too. He said to Little Red Riding Hood, “Hello, Little Red Riding Hood, I’m your grandmother, I live here.”

小红帽被这个生物的假扮欺骗了，感到很害怕。她对wolf说：“你不是我的外婆，你的眼睛很大很尖锐，我知道你是狼。”

Little Red Riding Hood was fooled by the creature’s disguise, and she felt very scared. She said to the Wolf, “You’re not my grandmother, your eyes are very big and sharp, I know you’re a wolf.”

wolf很生气，感到很恼怒。他对小红帽说：“你说我是狼，我就是狼！我会吃掉你！”然后，他跑向小红帽，准备吃掉她。

The Wolf was very angry, feeling very annoyed. He said to Little Red Riding Hood, “You say I’m a wolf, I’m a wolf! I’ll eat you!” Then, he ran towards Little Red Riding Hood, preparing to eat her.

第二章：小红帽的智慧和勇敢（Chapter 2: Little Red Riding Hood’s Intelligence and Bravery）

小红帽看到wolf的脸部很近，感到很害怕。她迅速地回忆妈妈留给她的信，发现其中有一句话：“如果遇到危险，跑回家。”她对wolf说：“我会跑回家，我不想被你吃掉！”然后，她转身跑回家。

Little Red Riding Hood saw the Wolf’s face very close, and she felt very scared. She quickly recalled the letter her mother had left for her, discovering a sentence that said, “If you meet danger, run back home.” She said to the Wolf, “I’ll run back home, I don’t want to be eaten by you!” Then, she turned around and ran back home.

wolf感到很恼怒，感到很失望。但是，他也感到很好奇，为什么小红帽可以跑回家。他决定跟随小红帽，想要知道她的秘密。

The Wolf felt very annoyed and disappointed, but he also felt very curious, wondering why Little Red Riding Hood could run back home. He decided to follow Little Red Riding Hood, wanting to know her secret.

小红帽发现wolf跟随她，感到很害怕。她继续跑步，直到来到家门口。她打开门，进去告诉妈妈发生了什么事。

Little Red Riding Hood discovered the Wolf was following her, and she felt very scared. She continued running, until she reached her home door. She opened the door and went inside, telling her mother what had happened.

妈妈非常生气，感到很愤怒。她对wolf说：“你是一个很坏的生物，你无法骗过我的女儿！”然后，她拿起一根棍子，准备打狼。

Her mother was very angry, feeling very furious. She said to the Wolf, “You’re a very bad creature, you can’t fool my daughter!” Then, she picked up a stick, preparing to hit the Wolf.

wolf见到妈妈的棍子，感到很害怕。他跑回森林，决不再骗过任何人。

The Wolf saw the mother’s stick, and he felt very scared. He ran back to the forest, deciding never to deceive anyone again.

从那天开始，小红帽和她的妈妈更加紧密地相互依靠。他们一起度过了许多快乐的日子，度过了许多危险的夜晚。

From that day on, Little Red Riding Hood and her mother became even closer, relying on each other more tightly. They spent many happy days together, and many scary nights together.

结语（Conclusion）

小红帽的夜行之旅告诉我们，智慧和勇敢是非常重要的。我们需要学习如何保护自己，如何面对危险，如何与他人相互依靠。小红帽的故事也告诉我们，家庭的爱和依靠是非常重要的。

Little Red Riding Hood’s midnight journey teaches us that intelligence and bravery are very important. We need to learn how to protect ourselves, how to face danger, and how to rely on others. Little Red Riding Hood’s story also teaches us that family love and reliance are very important.
