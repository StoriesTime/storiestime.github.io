It had been ten years since the collapse, and the once-thriving streets of Manhattan were now a barren wasteland, littered with the remnants of a bygone era. Jack's office, a towering skyscraper in the heart of the financial district, stood as a haunting testament to the market's former glory. As he surveyed the desolate landscape, Jack's mind wandered back to the events that led to the collapse. It was the year 2050, and the global economy was on the brink of a new era of prosperity.
