They are not part of any known star cluster or galaxy, yet they appear to be moving in a predetermined pattern across the night sky. From her observatory nestled in the mountains of Arizona, Sophia would often stay up late, gazing through her telescope at the starry canvas above, searching for any clues that might lead her to the solution of the mystery. One night, as she was examining the movements of a particular star, Sophia noticed something peculiar. "It seems that these stars are reflecting the positions of the Earth's oceans, as well as the alignments of the planetary axis." Sophia gasped.
