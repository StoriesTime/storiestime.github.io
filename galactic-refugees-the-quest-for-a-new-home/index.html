Galactic Refugees: The Quest for a New Home


The cries of the desperate echoed through the sterile corridors of the Last Hope's refuge, a hulking sanctuary built to accommodate the tenacious survivors of a war-torn galaxy. It had been three cycles since the ignominious fall of their homeworld, Xanthea-IV. The once-thriving planet was now nothing but a radiating remnant of what had been, its atmosphere incinerated by the very belligerents that had proliferated across its once-sundered firmament. Amidst Devastation In the dubious heart of the refuge, the once-respected cyberneticist, Akira Kel'Randren stood rapidly pacing the slim strip of energetic blocks installed to furnish safety.
