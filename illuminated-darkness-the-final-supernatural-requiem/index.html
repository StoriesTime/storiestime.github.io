Illuminated Darkness: The Final Supernatural Requiem


In the shadow of the ancient, towering Gothic cathedral, an unspeakable darkness cast its inky tendrils across the once-verdished lands. The arcane energy that was its lifeblood pulsed within the depths of the celestial stone, yearning to break free. It was the heart of the cathedral, the living breath of the grotesque, otherworldly entity that slumbered there - Illuminated Darkness.

As the winter moon peeked through the clouds, its cold light reflecting off the hoarfrost that clung to the blackened spires, a bend in the time-space continuum would occur. Families would hear the forbidden whispers, the muffled echoes, and wailing lamentations that pervaded the night. A supernatural requiem like no other; the final requiem, born of humanity’s darkest hour, would unlock the gate of Illuminated Darkness to take its place among the celestial bodies - a monstrous specter of a world painted by order and chaos.

The main protagonist, Raven, was no stranger to the whispers, the sinister gleams from shadows that seemed to come alive under the tranquil moonlight, and the lurking fear that gnawed at the back of his mind. Four generations of his family had been cursed to bear witness to this spectral phenomenon that would soon herald the age of Illuminated Darkness.

Raven descended the steps of the old, crumbling family manor, where century-old portraits of his ancestors stared out at him - the same damp, hollow eyes and a sense of despair hung heavy in the air. As he walked down a narrow alley, the cathedral’s dark spires pierced the starless night sky. A sense of urgency in his heart pushed him forward, fueled by an inexplicable desire to confront the supernatural darkness barring the way to salvation.

As he neared the cathedral’s entrance, his fingertips grazed the ancient iron lock. It released its hold and swung inward, the door creaking open as if beckoning him to enter. The darkness within reached out and swallowed Raven, and as he stepped over the threshold, he felt the weight of numerous souls that had fallen to the unspeakable horrors of Illuminated Darkness.

A scream tore through the hallowed silence of the cathedral. A silhouette detached itself from the omnipresent shadows, merging into Raven’s form. It whispered words antique and vile, promising power, destruction, and eternal dominion. Yet, in its monstrous nature, it revealed the true essence of Raven - one who could wield the darkness.

Together, they ventured into the crypts beneath the now forsaken hallowed ground. Torches flared to life within the darkness; every flicker aggravated the malevolent force hibernating in the depths. And it awakened, claiming the twisted path that awaited them - a living labyrinth with walls that changed shape, vaguely reminiscent of the once illustrious halls that marked the bishops’ entrance into the inner recesses of the cathedral.

Within these ever-shifting chambers, Raven’s metamorphosis reached its zenith. A new aura shone, one not merely of darkness, but also of purpose. His blade flashed with ethereal light, a polarization of his own soul. Within his grasp, he found a hidden key that he had kept from the entity. Their pact complete, they would seal away the so-called requiem of the world and reestablish the balance.

Walking through the shadows of the cathedral, Raven, illuminated darkness entwined, emerged from the labyrinthine depths and faced salvation. The demon that was once darkness lay at his feet, writhing in anguish. A single moment of unfeeling monotony; and as the shadows of Illuminated Darkness were finally consumed, Raven exhaled a ghostly lamentation to mark the end of the supernaturally-requiem.

Mankind, awakening from their slumber in torment, found a whole world reborn, gifted with a bizarre sense of harmony. The requiem had come - and gone. Curiously transformed, Cinderella would take her final bow in the twilight of the old world while the new arose like the morning sun over the spectral cathedral.
