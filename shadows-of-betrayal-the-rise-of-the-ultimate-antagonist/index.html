justice. The proceedings were grave and solemn, with Vin inoc serving as the chief witness against Aulinya. The evidence presented demonstrated the extent of Aulinya's deception, and the people of Ashwood began to realize the full extent of the damage his betrayal had caused. The trial was a somber affair, with many members of the community present to bear witness to the proceedings. Aulinya, once a beloved figure, stood quietly, his eyes cast downward in shame. The weight of his actions was crushing, and the people of Ashwood struggled to reconcile their love for him with the atrocities he had committed. In the end, the verdict was clear: Aulinya was found guilty of high treason and malfeasance, his crimes so serious that they would be punished with banishment from the land. As he was led away in chains, the people of Ashwood felt a mix of emotions: sorrow, anger, and a sense of relief that justice had been served. With Aulinya's removal from power, the people of Ashwood began to rebuild and restore their faith in their leaders. The once-maligned Vin inoc was hailed as a hero for his bravery in speaking out against Aulinya's corruption. His actions had saved the community from even greater suffering, and for that, he would always be remembered. But a new threat loomed on the horizon, one that would shake the foundations of Ashwood and threaten the very fabric of their society. In the shadows, a figure began to stir, fueled by a hunger for power and a desire for revenge against those who had wronged them. This was no ordinary individual; they were the result of Aulinya's corruption and his darker influences. Their name was Zatos, and they would stop at nothing to claim the position that Aulinya had lost. With their sights set on the top, Zatos was the shadowy figure who would one day become the most feared and reviled name in all of Ashwood. A new era of darkness loomed, one that would bring about a great reckoning.
