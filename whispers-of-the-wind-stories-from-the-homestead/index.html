In the rolling hills of the countryside, where the air is sweet with the scent of blooming wildflowers, and the sky stretches far and wide, there stands a small homestead. It's a place of enchantment, where the whispers of the wind carry the secrets of yesteryear. The homestead has been in the family for generations, and each member has contributed their own chapter to its ever-growing saga. Her father, a hardworking farmer, toiled day and night to provide for their small family. Elizabeth's childhood was spent gathered around the fireplace, listening to her father's stories of the war and his experiences on the battlefield. Her mother, a gentle soul with a kind heart, would freshen her needlepoint and so, every summer evening, the family sat on the porch, watching the stars come alive in the vast horizon.
