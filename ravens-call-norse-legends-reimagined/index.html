Raven's Call: Norse Legends Reimagined


The Golden Age of Nidavellir had long been a memory, a time when the Einherjar fought in battle with honor and valor. The Aesirds, bound to the great hammer Mjolnir, wielded the power of the Nine Worlds. But the Allfather Odin had fallen, and so began the battle for the future of Asgard. Within the forgotten reaches of the realm, a young Viking girl named Varda stumbled upon an ancient manuscript. Her worn leather armor and battle-scarred belt story told of a warrior and a nation forged through fire and steel. The worn pages seemed to whisper her name as she opened the worn covers, the letters within ending in the there word "Nyxhoppe" or Battle runs of Chaos. Varda's journey takes her through the mystical gates of Jotunheim, where titans and giants stomp their rolling thunder pounding across the horizon. Among hysteria she yearns to reunite the weakened Asgard and restore order in the Vikings fragmented realm of realm mighty Fenrir's prophesied downfall.
