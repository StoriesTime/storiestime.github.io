Beyond Boundaries: Stories of Aspiring Souls


As the steam train lumbered its way through the heart of the sprawling metropolis, among the cacophony of roars from machines and murmurs from people, sat Alida. Adorned in a rich sky-blue dress she watched as streets fascinated her, it felt like witnessing a symphony for the first time. Her mossy green eyes darted from left to right, not missing a single visual delight. New footpaths glistered and gleamed in the sun; shops were festooned with vibrant, tempting colours, and the air was dense with a smell of fragrant blooms, intoxicating and exotic.

Alida was here, not just watching and breathing, but experiencing the vivacity of another world she had longed for. She thought of her quaint little village, nothing if not picturesque and humble. How would it benefit from the marvelous inventions known to her this dazzling city had? Alida was here to find the answer.

Chapter 2: The Dreamer

Alida was in no haste to participate in the chaotic commotion of the city. Instead, she perched herself on a worn-out wooden bench nestled near the park lush with flowerbeds. It was in this humble corner that she spent time dreaming, a habit she had honed from birth.

The park was a favourite among many, beautiful greenery surrounded by a tall crumbling wall, which whispered tales of generations long gone. Lost in her dive into this ocean of thoughts, Alida wondered just how her seemingly ordinary village could reap the benefits of the city’s modern marvels.

A soft voice breached her engrossed thoughts. Startled, she turned to find a chalky-haired man in his mid-forties standing on her left. “You’re from the village, aren’t you?” he asked. Any other day, his tone may have startled her, but on this day, completing the connected thread of her thoughts, she nodded warily.

“I have a business proposition for you,” he mentioned, an elusive charm in his voice. His name, it seemed, was Danph, a shrewd merchant with a flair for adventure.

Chapter 3: A New Beginning

Danph introduced Alida to an entirely new world - a world where money disappeared into the thin air and reappeared triples the value in a jiffy. For a parched throat, it was nothing short of a miracle.

Alida suddenly realized - if capital was not confined, why should theons and their endless quantities be? Danph taught her that there was so much more to learn, far beyond the boundaries of her village. Yet it was she who must lead and shoulder all the heavy responsibilities.

The transformative journey of several years, tales of trials and tribulations, of life and death were nothing in comparison to the satisfaction of seeing her village transformed into what it is today, a splendid utopia.

Epilogue: The Aftermath

Alida barefoot walked on the freshly lain bricks of the temple road, the golden rays of the sun reflecting off the structure. Her village was unrecognizable; it blossomed, with this spiritual centre leading the transformation.

It all started with a train ride, a silent observer of the city’s thriving energy. As a coda to it all, Alida was glad to have ventured beyond her humble, quaint village, for it was in embracing the city’s allure that she truly learned to dream.

This was the tale of an ordinary woman who dared to reach for the stars, and in doing so, breathed life into the very essence of her beloved home. Beyond boundaries lies the story of aspiring souls, of hopes and dreams dusted off, reborn in vibrant colours of ambition, and most importantly, in unbridled belief of a future unconditional. Alida’s was one such story, and so were many others, each with a different ending yet spinning the same song of life.
