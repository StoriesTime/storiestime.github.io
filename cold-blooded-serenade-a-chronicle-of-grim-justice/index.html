Cold Blooded Serenade: A Chronicle of Grim Justice


In the shadowy alleyways of Neon City, rumors whispered of a recluse named Seren, famed within underground circles for his enigmatic ways and ruthless justice. Pale and emerald eyes reflected the crimson hue of the neon thatched above this treacherous metropolis, home to both influential crime lords and unbranded miscreants.

Unlike his contemporaries who tilted their blades with a scoffing smirk, Seren refrained from such theatrics. He granted quiet respect to his victims; they would understand his intent before regret ever took shape. It was said, within these exact alleyways, one fateful night, Seren was born anew with blood that shimmered like the first star that gives light to the endless abyss.

Young heir to the Thorne cartel, Silas, often ruminated over Seren’s remarkable obscurity despite his legendary reputation. In an odd twist of fate, Silas discovered Seren playing an entrancing serenade on his aged violin, nestled atop a cooling puddle of blood as if the melody siphoned life from it.

Seren’s haunting melody announced the sgian dubh, his cold blooded retribution, with a somber symmetry. Within his songs lay the echoes of many souls, the last whispers before they lost their breath. The melody drawn from the crimson-violin seeped into the listener’s bones, making them intimately aware of their sins before they faded away with the dissipating strains.

Silas shamelessly admitted he envied Seren for these hauntingly beautiful deeds to his name. An assassin with a conscience, who deemed murder not as a soulless act but as art, rising above petty vendettas. Intrigued, he searched for an opportunity to witness Seren in his element, and after weeks of covert surveillance, finally snuck into a secret underground gig at an abandoned factory.

Amidst a motley crew of ruffians and cartel members, Seren’s Serenade came to life. Crimson violin strings trembled to the rhythmic pulse of his iciness; the soundwave of impending doom echoed through the abandoned halls. Each chord drew forth the agony of hundreds of deaths and hundreds lives salvaged.

Witnesses shared stories of pacts Seren had brokered; contract killers, once foes, now bound in temporary truces. The mention of Seren’s name invoked not fear, but respect.

Perhaps sensing a supportive audience this night, Seren broke his cardinal rule by speaking. He opened his frost-covered lips as he rolled the bow across the strings, sharing his story with his listeners; the bleak tale of vengeance born from love.

It was said through war and suffering, emerald eyes that mirrored the brightest stars were born and in them lay a cold-blooded serenade for those lost to the tides of time.

As Seren played and sang that night, beneath the crimson glow of desperation, Silas contemplated the uncharted untapped avenues in his own saga for justice. As the melodies of Seren’s final song subsided, it sealed a pact; a mutual understanding to uphold the integrity of their deeds.

While the blooded path beneath Silas’ sandaled feet soaked up the tales whispered beneath unconscious lips, he sought his unique place among the shadow artists themselves. By the play of the crimson violin, Seren had created something daunting, beautiful, and ominous.

Yes, cold-blooded serenades would echo through the alleyways of Neon City that infamous night. Empowered by the knowing gaze of the crimson-eyed serpent who dared to weave his own melody from the most unremarkable of instruments… Blooded Scarlet Strings.
